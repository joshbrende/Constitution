[STEP] Why long-term trends matter

## Long-Term Trend Analysis

**Long-term trend analysis** uses **historical** and **projected** **trends** (demographic, economic, service, climate) to inform **IDP**, **infrastructure**, and **budget** planning. It supports **SALGA Outcome 2** (predictive capabilities) and **Outcome 5** (better planning). **Trends** help you: (1) **calibrate** **demand** and **revenue** **forecasts**; (2) **anticipate** **infrastructure** and **replacement** need; (3) **align** with **national** and **provincial** **priorities** (e.g. **climate**, **urbanisation**).

### Demographic trends

| Trend | What to look at | Use in planning |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Population** | **Growth rate**; **age** structure; **household** size. | **Demand** for **water**, **sanitation**, **waste**; **schools**, **health** (if applicable). |
| **Urbanisation** | **Rural → urban**; **informal** settlement **growth**. | **Spatial** plan; **bulk** and **reticulation**; **housing** and **services**. |
| **Migration** | **In / out** migration; **stats** or **proxy** (school, clinic). | **Demand** in **receiving** **areas**; **revenue** and **capacity**. |

**Sources:** **Stats SA**; **community survey**; **provincial** planning; **municipal** **estimates**. **AI** can help **suggest** **sources** and **how** to **translate** **trends** into **demand** **assumptions**—you **validate** with **Planning**.

[STEP] Economic and service trends

## Economic and Service Trends

- **Economic:** **GDP** or **GVA** **growth**; **employment**; **sector** mix (industry, retail, agriculture). Affects **revenue** (rates, services), **consumption**, and **ability to pay**. **National** and **provincial** **outlooks** often published.
- **Service:** **Historical** **connections**, **consumption**, **faults**, **backlog**. **Trend** (e.g. **growth** in **consumption** per connection) feeds **demand** **forecast**. Your **Module 7** **faults** and **Module 6** **revenue** **trackers** provide **data**.
- **Infrastructure lifecycle:** **Age** and **condition** of **assets**; **replacement** and **refurbishment** **cycles**. **Long-term** **trend** of **capex** need for **asset** **sustainability**. **AI** can help **suggest** **lifecycle** **assumptions** (e.g. **pump** **life** 20–25 years)—you **adapt** to **your** **asset** **register**.

[STEP] Climate and external trends

## Climate and External Trends

- **Climate:** **Drought**, **flood**, **temperature**; **water** **availability**; **energy** **demand** (heating, cooling); **extreme** **events**. **Long-term** **trends** and **scenarios** (e.g. **drier**) affect **demand**, **bulk**, and **resilience**. **National** and **sector** **climate** **strategies** give **direction**. **AI** can help **suggest** a **climate** **impact** **framework** (what to **consider**, what to **monitor**)—you **customise** and **validate** with **Planning** and **technical**.
- **External:** **Bulk** **price** (electricity, water); **grant** **policy**; **regulation**. **Trends** and **scenarios** help you **plan** for **changes**.

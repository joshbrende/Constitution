## Module 9: Key Takeaways

1. **Predictive modeling fundamentals:** **Predictive** = using **past** data and **assumptions** to **estimate** **future** (demand, revenue, population). **Types:** trend, **driver-based**, **scenario**, **expert**. **Limitations:** assumptions drive results; **data** quality; **black swans**. **State** assumptions; do **sensitivity**. **AI** can suggest **structures** and **assumptions**—you **validate** and **build** in **your** Excel or system.

2. **Demand forecasting:** **Drivers** by service: **population**, **households**, **connections**, **consumption**, **tariff**, **collection**. **Population** is a **master** driver; **Stats SA**, **community** **survey**, **provincial** are **sources**. **Link** to **IDP**, **budget**, **SDBIP**. **AI** can suggest **driver** **chains** and **ratios**—you **plug in** **your** **data** and **sources**.

3. **Resource allocation optimization:** **Demand** vs **capacity**; **gap** = **prioritised** **investment**. **Prioritisation:** IDP, **cost–benefit**, **risk**, **equity**, **multi-criteria**. **Link** to **MTREF** and **budget**. **AI** can suggest **criteria** and **weights**—**council** **approves**.

4. **Scenario planning:** **Base**, **upside**, **downside** by **varying** **key** **assumptions**. **Use** in **IDP**, **MTREF**, **council** **stress-testing**. **Sensitivity:** one **variable** at a time. **AI** can suggest **assumption** **tables** and **narrative**—you **populate** and **validate** with **Planning** and **CFO**.

5. **Long-term trend analysis:** **Demographic** (population, **urbanisation**, **migration**); **economic** (growth, **employment**); **service** (connections, **consumption**, **faults**); **infrastructure** **lifecycle** (age, **replacement**); **climate** (drought, **flood**, **temperature**). **Sources:** Stats SA, **sector**, **national**/ **provincial**. **AI** can suggest **frameworks** and **assumptions**—you **customise** and **validate**.

6. **Practical skills:** You can **build** a **demand** **forecast** **worksheet** (Assumptions, Demand by service, **link** to **IDP**/ **SDBIP**) and a **scenario** **planning** **template** (assumptions **base**/ **upside**/ **downside**, **results**, **narrative**). Both support **IDP**, **MTREF**, and **council** **decisions**.

7. **AI Application—data and ethics:** Use **aggregated** **only** (e.g. "population 250k," "growth 1.5%"). **Never** **confidential** or **person-specific** in public AI. **Validate** and **approve** with **Planning** and **CFO**. **AI** suggests **structures**, **drivers**, and **narrative**—you **own** the **model**, **assumptions**, and **council** **submissions**.

## Module 9: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Predictive and demand:** Does your **IDP** or **MTREF** use **demand** **forecasts** (population, connections, consumption)? What **drivers** and **sources** do you use? Would a **demand** **worksheet** (from the Practical) help—and who would **own** and **approve** the **assumptions**?

2. **Resource allocation:** How does your municipality **prioritise** **capex** and **O&M** when **resources** are **limited**? Is it **explicit** (criteria, **weights**) or **implicit**? Would **AI**-suggested **prioritisation** **criteria** help—and how would **council** **engage** (e.g. **workshop**, **IDP** **consultation**)?

3. **Scenario planning:** Do you **stress-test** the **budget** or **IDP** with **upside**/ **downside** **scenarios**? What **key** **assumptions** do you **vary** (growth, collection, grant, bulk)? Would a **scenario** **template** (from the Practical) help—and who **validates** (Planning, CFO, both)?

4. **Long-term trends:** Do you **use** **demographic**, **economic**, or **climate** **trends** in **planning**? What **sources** (Stats SA, provincial, **sector**)? Would **AI**-suggested **climate** **impact** or **lifecycle** **frameworks** help—and who would **customise** (Planning, **technical**)?

5. **AI and approval:** For the **AI Application** tasks: did you use **only** **aggregated** data? What would you need (e.g. **Planning** and **CFO** **sign-off**, **template** for **assumption** **narratives**) to make **AI**-assisted **demand** and **scenario** **planning** **routine** for **IDP** or **MTREF**?

## Module 12: Key Takeaways

1. **Action plan development:** **Individual** or **team** **plans** turn **Module 11** **roadmap** and **P1–P4** into **concrete** **actions**. **Structure**: **Action**, **Owner**, **Due** **date**, **Resources**, **Success** **metric**, **Dependencies**. **Link** to **SDBIP** and **initiatives** for **council** and **audit** **alignment**. **SALGA Outcome 1** (action-oriented) and **Outcome 3** (implementation readiness) require **plans** that are **executable** and **held** to **account**.

2. **Prioritisation of AI initiatives:** **Select** from **Module 11** **initiatives** **list** and **P1–P4** for the **first** **3–6** **months**. **Aim** for **3–6** **actions** (individual) or **5–12** (team); **at** **least** **one** **Quick** **win**; **respect** **dependencies** and **realistic** **capacity**. **If** **Module 11** was **skipped**, **pick** **2–5** **initiatives** from **Modules 2–10** as **P1**.

3. **Resource identification:** **Who**: **owner**, **champion**, **support**. **What**: **budget**, **tools**, **data** **access**, **approvals**, **skills**. **Where**: **CFO**/ **IT**/ **data** **owners**/ **governance**. **Document** **gaps** as **dependencies** or **risks**; **escalate** as **needed**.

4. **Timeline development:** **Due** **dates** and **milestones** per **action**; **order** by **dependencies**. **Consider** **council** **cycles**, **leave**, **procurement**. **Realistic** **first** **steps** = **1–2** **actions** that can **start** **immediately** to **build** **momentum**.

5. **Success metrics definition:** **One** **clear** **metric** per **action** ( **output** or **outcome**); **avoid** **vague** "**done**". **Review** **at** **due** **date** and **plan-level** ( **monthly**/ **quarterly**); **learn** and **adapt**; **link** to **roadmap** **review**.

6. **Practical skills:** You **built** an **action** **plan** **template** ( **columns**, **P1** **actions**, **What** **needs** **attention**) and **completed** **peer** **review** ( **give** and **receive** **feedback**; **record** and **apply**). **Plan** is **ready** for **Course** **Closure** **presentations** and **take-back** to your **municipality**.

7. **AI Application—data and validation:** **Generic** or **aggregated** **inputs** only; **never** **confidential** **budget**, **personnel**, or **identifiable** **data** in **public** **AI**. **AI** **assists**: **action** **plan** **generation** from **priorities**, **template-based** **planning**, **peer** **review** **questions**, **expert-style** **feedback**, **plan** **validation** **checklist**. **You** **edit**, **validate** with **owner** or **MM**, and **own** the **final** **plan**.

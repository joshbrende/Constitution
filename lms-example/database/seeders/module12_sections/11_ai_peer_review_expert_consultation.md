## AI Application: Peer Review and Expert Consultation

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**AI** can **support** **peer** **review** and **expert-style** **feedback** by: (1) **generating** **peer** **review** **questions** or **checklists** you **use** when **reviewing** **someone** **else's** **plan**; (2) **playing** "**expert** **consultant**": you **paste** a **generic** **summary** of your **plan** ( **no** **confidential** **or** **identifiable** **data**), and **AI** **suggests** **strengths**, **gaps**, and **improvements**. **Human** **peer** **review** ( **Section** **8**) **remains** **primary**—**AI** **complements** with **extra** **lenses** and **structure**. **You** **own** all **decisions**; **validate** **AI** **feedback** with **plan** **owner** or **MM** before **adoption**.

[STEP] Task A — Generate peer review questions

### Task A: Generate Peer Review Questions

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We are **reviewing** **municipal** **action** **plans** for **AI** **implementation** ( **first** **3–6** **months**). Suggest **8–10** **peer** **review** **questions** in **categories**: (1) **Clarity** and **specificity** of **actions**; (2) **Realism** of **dates** and **resources**; (3) **Completeness** ( **owner**, **metric**, **dependencies**); (4) **Alignment** with **priorities** and **Quick** **wins**. We will use these when **reviewing** each **other's** plans—we are NOT putting our plans into AI.

3. **Use the output:** **Adopt** or **adapt** as **checklist** for **Section** **8** **peer** **review**; **tick** as you **review**. **AI** **suggests**; **you** **run** the **review**.

[STEP] Task B — Expert consultation (generic summary only)

### Task B: Expert Consultation (Generic Summary Only)

1. In a **new** chat, write a **short** **generic** **summary** of your **plan**. **Example** ( **no** **names**, **figures**, **or** **identifiers**):

   > Our **action** **plan**: **3** **priorities**—**prompt** **library**, **data** **inventory** for **revenue**, **revenue** **tracker**. **First** **action** **due** in **6** **weeks**; **data** **inventory** **depends** on **IT** **access**; **success** **metrics** are **sign-off** and **weekly** **usage**. **Resources**: **Excel**, **existing** **data** **owner**; **one** **approval** **pending**.

2. Send:

   > Acting as an **expert** **consultant** on **municipal** **implementation**, review this **action** **plan** **summary**. Give: (1) **one** **strength**; (2) **one** **or** **two** **potential** **gaps** or **risks**; (3) **one** **suggestion** to **strengthen** **it**. Be **brief**. We will **verify** with our **team**—we are NOT putting confidential data into AI.

3. **Use the output:** **Consider** **suggestions**; **add** to **peer** **review** **notes** or **"What** **needs** **attention"**. **Do** **not** **paste** **real** **names**, **budget**, or **identifiable** **detail**—**AI** **expert** **role** is **structure** and **prompts** only; **you** **own** **content**.

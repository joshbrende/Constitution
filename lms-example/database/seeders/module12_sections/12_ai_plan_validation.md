## AI Application: Plan Validation

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**Plan** **validation** checks that your **action** **plan** is **ready** to **present** or **implement**: **SMART** **actions**, **owners** **assigned**, **resources** **identified**, **dates** **realistic**, **metrics** **defined**. **AI** can: (1) **generate** a **validation** **checklist** ( **yes/no** or **short** **criteria**); (2) **review** a **generic** **summary** of your **plan** against that **checklist** and **suggest** **fixes**. You **run** the **check** on **your** **full** **plan**; **use** **only** **generic** or **aggregated** **summaries** in **AI**—**never** **confidential** **personnel**, **budget**, or **vendor** **detail**. **Validate** **final** **plan** with **plan** **owner** or **MM** before **council** or **SDBIP**.

[STEP] Task A — Generate a plan validation checklist

### Task A: Generate a Plan Validation Checklist

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We need a **validation** **checklist** for a **municipal** **action** **plan** ( **AI** **implementation**, **first** **3–6** **months**). Suggest **10–12** **yes/no** or **short** **criteria** in **categories**: (1) **Actions** ( **SMART**, **specific**); (2) **Owners** ( **assigned** for **each**); (3) **Resources** ( **identified** or **escalated**); (4) **Dates** ( **realistic**, **dependencies** **ordered**); (5) **Success** **metrics** ( **defined** per **action**); (6) **Alignment** ( **link** to **priorities**, **Quick** **win** in **first** **3** **months**). We will **run** this **ourselves** on our **plans**—we are NOT putting our data into AI.

3. **Use the output:** **Turn** into a **checklist** ( **tick** **boxes**); **run** against your **action** **plan**. **Fix** any **"No"** **before** **finalising**. **AI** **suggests**; **you** **own** the **check** and **plan**.

[STEP] Task B — Validate a generic plan summary

### Task B: Validate a Generic Plan Summary

1. In a **new** chat, provide a **short** **generic** **summary**. **Example**:

   > **Action** **plan** **summary**: **4** **actions**—**prompt** **library** **list** ( **owner** **PMU**); **data** **inventory** ( **owner** **CFO**; **depends** on **IT**); **revenue** **tracker** ( **after** **inventory**); **pilot** **prompt** **use** ( **3** **staff**). **Due** **dates** **over** **4** **months**. **Metrics**: **sign-off**, **weekly** **usage**. **Resource** **gap**: **IT** **access** **TBC**.

2. Send:

   > Against a **good** **action** **plan** **validation** **checklist**, does this **summary** **pass**? List: (1) **What** **passes**; (2) **What** **might** **fail** or **need** **strengthening**; (3) **One** **or** **two** **concrete** **fixes**. Brief. We will **apply** to our **full** **plan**—we are NOT putting confidential data into AI.

3. **Use the output:** **Address** **gaps** in your **real** **plan**; **re-run** **checklist** if **needed**. **AI** **reviews** **structure**; **you** **own** **content** and **sign-off**.

## When to Use Which AI Task

| Module 12 area | AI task | What you get | What you must do |
|----------------|---------|--------------|------------------|
| **Action plan generation** | Draft **actions** from **priorities**; **dependencies** and **order** | **Table** of **actions**, **owners** ( **roles**), **resources**, **metrics** | **Edit**; **add** **real** **names** and **dates**; **never** **confidential** **data** in **AI**. |
| **Template-based planning** | **Action** **plan** **template**; **90‑day** **structure** | **Columns**, **examples**, **monthly** **focus** | **Use** in **Excel**/ **Sheets**; **populate** with **your** **data**. |
| **Peer review / expert** | **Peer** **review** **questions**; **expert** **feedback** on **generic** **summary** | **Checklist**; **strengths**, **gaps**, **suggestions** | **Run** **peer** **review**; **consider** **AI** **suggestions**; **human** **review** **primary**. |
| **Plan validation** | **Validation** **checklist**; **review** of **generic** **summary** | **Yes/no** **criteria**; **pass**/ **fail** **and** **fixes** | **Run** on **full** **plan**; **fix** **gaps**; **validate** with **owner** or **MM**. |

**Remember:** **AI** **assists** **structure**, **templates**, and **validation**—it does **not** **replace** **your** **ownership** of the **plan** or **accountability** to **MM** and **council**. **Aggregated** or **generic** **inputs** only; **validate** with **plan** **owner** before **Course** **Closure** **presentations**.

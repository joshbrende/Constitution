## Timeline Development

A **timeline** gives **when** each **action** will **happen** and **how** **actions** **relate** to **each** **other**. **Realistic** **dates** improve **accountability** and **avoid** **overload** in **one** **month**.

### Milestones and due dates

- **Due date per action:** **Specific** **date** (e.g. "31 **Mar** 2026") or **quarter** (e.g. "**Q1**") if **exact** **date** is **unknown**. **Avoid** "**TBC**" without a **review** **date** to **set** it.
- **Milestones:** **Key** **checkpoints** (e.g. "**Data** **mapping** **signed** **off**"; "**Pilot** **goes** **live**"). **Use** as **gates**—**next** **actions** **may** **depend** on **milestone** **achievement**.
- **First 3–6 months focus:** **Spread** **actions** so **at** **least** **one** **Quick** **win** **lands** in **months** **1–3**; **heavier** **foundational** **work** can **run** into **months** **4–6**.

[STEP] Dependencies and realistic first steps

## Dependencies and Realistic First Steps

| Consideration | Why it matters |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Dependencies** | **Action** B **cannot** **start** until **Action** A is **done** (e.g. **data** **access** **before** **tracker**). **Order** **due** **dates** so **A** **&lt; B**; **state** **dependency** in **plan**. |
| **Council and committee cycles** | **Approvals** ( **MM**, **council**, **audit**) **follow** **meeting** **schedules**. **Do** **not** **plan** **approval** in **Dec** if **council** **recess**. |
| **Leave and capacity** | **Peak** **reporting** (e.g. **SDBIP**, **audit** **prep**) **consumes** **capacity**. **Avoid** **overloading** **same** **weeks** as **major** **deadlines**. |
| **Budget and procurement** | **New** **licences** or **vendors** **need** **procurement** **cycles**. **Back** **date** "**Request** **approval**" **earlier** than "**Pilot** **start**". |

**Realistic** **first** **steps** = **1–2** **actions** that can **start** **immediately** (e.g. "**Draft** **prompt** **library** **list**"; "**Request** **billing** **data** **access**") with **no** **blockers**. **Build** **momentum**; **add** **dependent** **actions** **after**.

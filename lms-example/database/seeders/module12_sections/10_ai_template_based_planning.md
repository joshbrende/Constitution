## AI Application: Template-Based Planning

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**Templates** **standardise** **structure** and **speed** **planning**. **AI** can: (1) **generate** an **action** **plan** **template** ( **columns**, **headers**, **example** **rows**); (2) **suggest** a **90‑day** **planning** **structure** ( **Month** **1**, **2**, **3** **focus**); (3) **draft** **brief** **instructions** for **filling** it in. You **use** **templates** in **Excel**/ **Sheets**; **populate** with **your** **data**—**never** **confidential** **detail** in **public** **AI**. **Validate** **templates** with **your** **team** or **MM** if **adopting** **organisation‑wide**.

[STEP] Task A — Action plan template

### Task A: Action Plan Template

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We need an **action** **plan** **template** for a **municipal** **AI** **implementation** ( **first** **3–6** **months**). Provide: (1) **Column** **headers** with **short** **definitions**; (2) **2–3** **example** **rows** ( **generic**—e.g. "**Draft** **prompt** **library**", "**Data** **inventory**") to show **how** to **fill** each **column**; (3) **one** **paragraph** of **instructions** for **users**. Format so we can **paste** into **Excel** or **Sheets**. We will add our own actions—we are NOT putting confidential data into AI.

3. **Use the output:** **Create** or **refine** your **Action** **Plan** **sheet**; **ensure** **columns** match **Module 12** **Practical** ( **Action**, **Owner**, **Due** **date**, **Resources**, **Success** **metric**, **Dependencies**). **AI** **suggests** **structure**; **you** **own** the **template** and **content**.

[STEP] Task B — 90‑day planning structure

### Task B: 90‑Day Planning Structure

1. In a **new** chat, send:

   > For a South African municipality starting **AI** **initiatives** (e.g. **prompt** **library**, **data** **mapping**, **revenue** **tracker**), suggest a **90‑day** **planning** **structure**: **Month** **1** focus (e.g. **set‑up**, **approvals**, **first** **deliverable**); **Month** **2** focus; **Month** **3** focus. One **table** with **Month** | **Focus** | **Example** **actions** | **Key** **milestone**. Generic only—we will adapt to our **priorities** and **capacity**. We are NOT putting our data into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Map** your **actions** to **Month** **1–3**; **ensure** **at** **least** **one** **Quick** **win** **milestone** in **Month** **1–2**. **AI** **proposes** **rhythm**; **you** **assign** **dates** and **owners**.

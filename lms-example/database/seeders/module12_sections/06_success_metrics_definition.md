[STEP] What to measure for each action

## Success Metrics Definition

**Success** **metrics** answer: **how** will you **know** each **action** or **initiative** **succeeded**? **Good** **metrics** are **specific**, **measurable**, and **owned**. **SALGA** and **council** **expect** **evidence** of **implementation**—**metrics** provide it.

### What to measure per action

| Action type | Example success metric |
|-------------|------------------------|
| **Deliverable** (e.g. **inventory**, **tracker**, **checklist**) | "**Document** **signed** **off** by [owner]"; "**Tracker** **live** with **3** **KPIs** **updated** **weekly**." |
| **Pilot** (e.g. **prompt** **library**, **chatbot**) | "**3** **staff** **using** **weekly**"; "**10** **queries** **handled** by **chatbot** in **pilot** **month**." |
| **Process** (e.g. **training**, **governance** **approval**) | "**5** **staff** **trained**"; "**AI** **use** **policy** **approved** by **MM**." |
| **Outcome** (e.g. **efficiency**, **quality**) | "**Hours** **saved** **per** **report** **&lt; X**"; "**Variance** **narrative** **review** **time** **reduced** **by** **Y%**." **Baseline** **before** **start**; **measure** **after**. |

**Prefer** **one** **clear** **metric** **per** **action**; **add** **a** **second** if **output** and **outcome** **both** matter. **Avoid** **vague** "**Complete**" or "**Done**"—**define** what "**done**" **looks** **like**.

[STEP] When to review

## When to Review

- **Per action:** **Review** **at** **due** **date** ( **done** / **not** **done**; **metric** **met** / **not** **met**). **If** **not** **done**, **record** **reason** and **new** **date**.
- **Plan-level:** **Review** **all** **actions** **monthly** ( **team** or **owner**) or **quarterly** ( **MM**/ **steering**). **Update** **timeline** and **priorities**; **link** to **Module 11** **roadmap** **review**.
- **Learning:** **What** **worked**? **What** **blocked**? **Use** **insights** to **adjust** **next** **actions** and **feed** **back** into **roadmap** ( **P1–P4** **reprioritisation**).

**AI** can help **suggest** **metrics** or **review** **questions**—you **choose** and **own** what you **measure**. **Never** put **confidential** **performance** **data** into **public** **AI**.

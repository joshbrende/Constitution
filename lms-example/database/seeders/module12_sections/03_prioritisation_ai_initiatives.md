## Prioritisation of AI Initiatives

**Module 12** **action** **plans** **draw** **from** your **Module 11** **roadmap** and **prioritisation** (P1–P4). You **cannot** **do** **everything** in **3–6** **months**—**prioritisation** helps you **choose** **what** to **put** in your **action** **plan** and **what** to **defer**.

### Using Module 11 outputs

| Module 11 output | How to use it in Module 12 |
|------------------|----------------------------|
| **Initiatives list** | **Source** of **actions**. Each **initiative** (e.g. "**Prompt** **library**", "**Data** **mapping**", "**Revenue** **tracker**") can become **1–3** **actions** with **owners** and **dates**. |
| **Phases** (Quick win / Foundational / Advanced) | **First** **3–6** **months**: focus on **Quick** **wins** and **early** **Foundational** **items**. **Advanced** usually **later** (e.g. **6–12** **months**). |
| **P1–P4** **prioritisation** | **P1** = **do** **first** → **top** **of** **action** **plan**. **P2** = **next** **3–6** **months** or **when** **P1** **done**. **P3–P4** = **defer** or **include** as "**Review** **in** **Q3**" **only**. |
| **Dependencies** | **Actions** that **depend** on **others** (e.g. "**Data** **mapping**" **before** "**Revenue** **tracker**") **must** be **ordered** **correctly** in **timeline** and **dates**. |

## Selecting for the First 3–6 Months

**Guidance** for **what** to **include** in your **action** **plan**:

- **Aim for 3–6 actions** (individual) or **5–12** (team). **Fewer**, **done** **well**, beats **many** **half‑done**.
- **At least one Quick win** from Module 11 (e.g. **prompt** **library**, **checklist** **pilot**, **drafting** **support**)—**builds** **trust** and **visibility** in **&lt;3** **months**.
- **Respect dependencies:** if "**Data** **mapping**" is **P1** and **blocks** **Revenue** **tracker**, **put** **data** **mapping** **first** with an **earlier** **due** **date**.
- **Realistic:** **check** **leave**, **council** **cycles**, **budget** **approval** **timing**. **Do** **not** **overload** **Q1** if **approvals** are **in** **March**.
- **If you skipped or simplified Module 11:** **pick** **2–5** **initiatives** from **Modules 2–10** you **will** **do** in **3–6** **months** (e.g. **indicator** **extraction**, **one‑page** **dashboard**, **compliance** **tracker**) and **treat** them as **P1** for this **plan**.

**SALGA Outcome 1** (action-oriented) and **Outcome 3** (implementation readiness) are **strengthened** when **priorities** are **clear** and **first** **steps** are **achievable**.

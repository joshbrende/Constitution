## Practical: Peer Review and Feedback

**Peer** **review** gives you **feedback** on your **action** **plan** **before** you **finalise** and **take** it **back** to your **municipality**. **Another** **participant** (or **facilitator**) reviews **clarity**, **realism**, **completeness**, and **alignment** with **Module 11**. **SALGA Outcome 4** (Effective Networks) is **reinforced** by **peer** **learning** and **shared** **standards**.

### What to give and receive

| You give (as reviewer) | You receive (as reviewee) |
|------------------------|----------------------------|
| **Clarity:** Are **actions** **specific** and **understandable**? | **Suggestions** to **sharpen** **wording** ( **SMART**). |
| **Realism:** Are **due** **dates** and **resources** **achievable**? **Dependencies** **identified**? | **Risks** or **gaps** you **may** have **missed**. |
| **Completeness:** **Owner**, **success** **metric**, **resources** for **each** **action**? | **Missing** **columns** or **logic** **gaps**. |
| **Alignment:** **Link** to **Module 11** **P1**/ **roadmap** **clear**? **Quick** **win** **in** **first** **3** **months**? | **Ideas** to **better** **align** with **SDBIP** or **council** **priorities**. |

**Be** **constructive**: **one** **or** **two** **specific** **improvements** per **theme**; **avoid** **vague** "**looks** **good**" or **harsh** **criticism** without **alternatives**.

## How to Run the Exchange and Record It

### Step 1: Pair and swap

1. **Pair** with **another** **participant** (or **in** **threes** with **rotating** **reviewer**).
2. **Swap** **action** **plans** ( **sheet** or **printed** **summary**). **5** **minutes** to **read** in **silence**.

### Step 2: Structured feedback (5–7 minutes per plan)

**Reviewer** **answers** ( **aloud** or **in** **writing**):

1. **What** is **strongest**? ( **one** **or** **two** **points**)
2. **What** could be **clearer** or **more** **specific**? ( **one** **or** **two** **actions**)
3. **What** **dependency**, **resource**, or **date** might be **unrealistic** or **missing**?
4. **One** **suggestion** to **strengthen** **alignment** with **Module 11** or **SDBIP**.

**Reviewee** **takes** **notes**; **does** **not** **argue**—**clarify** **only** if **needed**.

### Step 3: Record and apply

- **Reviewee** **records** **feedback** in **one** **of** **these** **ways**:
  - **Footer** or **new** **sheet** "**Peer** **review** **notes**": **bullet** **points** of **suggestions** and **whether** you will **adopt** ( **Yes** / **Partially** / **No**—**reason**).
  - **Or** **direct** **edits** to the **plan** with **short** **note** "**From** **peer** **review**: [change]."
- **Apply** **at** **least** **1–2** **changes** before **finalising**; **note** **any** you **reject** and **why** ( **for** **your** **own** **discipline**).

**AI** ( **sections** **9–12**) can **support** **peer** **review** **questions** and **validation**—**human** **peer** **feedback** **remains** **central** for **this** **workshop**.

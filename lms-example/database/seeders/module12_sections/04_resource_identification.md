## Resource Identification

**Actions** **fail** when **resources** are **missing** ( **owner**, **time**, **data**, **approval**). **Identifying** **resources** **up** **front** makes your **action** **plan** **credible** and **executable**. **SALGA Outcome 3** (Capable Local Government) implies **realistic** **resourcing** for **implementation**.

### Who

| Role | What they do | Example |
|------|--------------|---------|
| **Owner** | **Accountable** for **delivery**; **reports** **progress**. | **PMU** **manager** for "**Indicator** **extraction** **pilot**". |
| **Champion** | **Advocates**, **unblocks**, **supports** **adoption**. Often **senior** ( **MM**, **director**). | **CFO** **champions** **revenue** **tracker**; **MM** **champions** **AI** **governance**. |
| **Support** | **Provides** **data**, **IT**, **approvals**, **training**. | **IT** for **data** **access**; **HR** for **training** **slots**. |

**For** **each** **action**, **name** or **identify** **owner**; **note** **champion** and **support** if **needed** so **nothing** is **assumed**.

## What and Where

| Resource type | What to identify | Where to find it |
|---------------|------------------|------------------|
| **Budget** | **Licences** (e.g. **ChatGPT** **Team**), **vendor**, **training**, **internal** **time**. | **CFO**/ **Treasury**; **existing** **IT** or **PM** **budget**; **SDBIP** **projects**. |
| **Tools** | **Excel**/ **Sheets**, **BI** **tools**, **AI** **platforms**. | **IT** **catalog**; **existing** **licences**; **trial** **periods** for **new** **tools**. |
| **Data** | **Access** to **billing**, **complaints**, **SDBIP**, **assets**, etc. | **Data** **owner** ( **Revenue**, **Customer** **Care**, **PMU**); **Module 4** **data** **inventory**. |
| **Approvals** | **MM**, **council**, **section** **56**; **POPI**/ **governance** **sign-off**. | **Delegations**; **committee** **cycles**; **governance** **forum**. |
| **Skills** | **Training** (this **course**); **refreshers**; **champion** **time**. | **HR** **L&D**; **SALGA**/ **CoGTA** **offerings**; **internal** **champions**. |

**Document** **gaps** (e.g. "**Data** **access**—**pending** **IT**") as **dependencies** or **risks** in your **action** **plan** so they can be **escalated**.

## Module 12: Reflection Questions

Before **Course** **Closure** **&** **Certification**, consider:

1. **Action plan:** Is **every** **action** **specific** ( **SMART**), with **owner**, **due** **date**, **resources**, and **success** **metric**? **What** **one** **change** from **peer** **review** did you **adopt**—and **why**?

2. **Prioritisation:** Did you **select** from **Module 11** **P1–P4**—or **pick** **from** **Modules 2–10**? **Do** you have **at** **least** **one** **Quick** **win** in **&lt;3** **months**? **What** did you **defer** ( **P3**/ **P4**) and **when** will you **revisit**?

3. **Resources:** **Which** **resource** ( **budget**, **data** **access**, **approval**, **support**) is **most** **at** **risk** or **still** **TBC**? **Who** will you **escalate** to **first** **back** at the **municipality**?

4. **Timeline:** Are **due** **dates** **realistic** given **council** **cycles**, **leave**, and **capacity**? **What** **dependency** could **slip** and **delay** **other** **actions**—and **what** is your **mitigation**?

5. **Success metrics:** **Which** **action** has the **clearest** **success** **metric**—and **which** might **need** **sharpening** before you **present** or **share** with **MM**?

6. **AI and validation:** Did you **use** **AI** for **draft** **actions**, **templates**, **peer** **review** **questions**, or **validation** **checklist**? **What** **one** **AI** **suggestion** did you **use**—and **what** did you **reject** and **why**? **Who** will **validate** your **plan** before **council** or **SDBIP**?

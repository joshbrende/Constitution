## AI Application: Action Plan Generation

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**AI** can **help** **generate** **draft** **actions** from your **priorities** or **initiatives** **list**. You **provide** **generic** or **aggregated** **inputs** (e.g. "**P1**: **prompt** **library**, **data** **mapping**, **revenue** **tracker**")—**never** **confidential** **budget**, **personnel**, or **identifiable** **data**. **AI** **suggests** **actions**, **owners** ( **roles**), **typical** **resources**, **and** **success** **metrics**; **you** **edit**, **assign** **real** **names**, and **own** the **final** **plan**. **Validate** with **plan** **owner** or **MM** before **use** in **council** or **SDBIP**.

[STEP] Task A — Generate draft actions from priorities

### Task A: Generate Draft Actions from Priorities

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send ( **customise** **brackets** with **your** **priorities**—**generic** **only**):

   > We are a South African municipality. Our **AI** **priorities** for the **next** **3–6** **months** are: [e.g. **prompt** **library** for **PMU**; **data** **inventory** for **revenue**; **revenue** **tracker**]. For **each** **priority**, suggest **1–3** **concrete** **actions** in a **table** with columns: **Action**, **Suggested** **owner** ( **role** only), **Typical** **due** (e.g. **weeks** or **quarter**), **Resources** **needed**, **Success** **metric**. Use **SMART** where possible. We will **edit** and **assign** **real** **people**—we are NOT putting confidential data into AI.

3. **Use the output:** **Copy** **into** your **Action** **Plan** **template**; **replace** "**Suggested** **owner**" with **real** **names**/ **roles**; **adjust** **dates** and **resources** to **your** **context**; **add** **dependencies**. **AI** **proposes**; **you** **own** the **plan**.

[STEP] Task B — Suggest dependencies and order

### Task B: Suggest Dependencies and Order

1. In the **same** or **new** chat, send:

   > For these **municipal** **actions**—(1) **Draft** **prompt** **library** **list**; (2) **Complete** **revenue** **data** **inventory**; (3) **Build** **revenue** **tracker**; (4) **Pilot** **prompt** **library** with **3** **staff**—suggest **dependencies** (what must be done first) and a **recommended** **order** with **typical** **timing** (e.g. **weeks**). Use a **table**. We will adapt to our **context**—we are NOT putting our data into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Check** **your** **plan** **order** and **Dependencies** **column**; **add** or **correct** as **needed**. **AI** **suggests**; **you** **validate** with **your** **knowledge** of **IT**, **approvals**, and **capacity**.

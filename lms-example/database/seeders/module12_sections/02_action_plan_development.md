[STEP] Individual vs team action plans

## Action Plan Development

An **action** **plan** turns **strategic** **intent** (e.g. your **Module 11** **roadmap** and **P1** **priorities**) into **concrete** **steps**: **what** to **do**, **who** will **do** it, **when**, with **what** **resources**, and **how** you will **know** it **succeeded**. **SALGA Outcome 1** (Agile Force—action-oriented) and **Outcome 3** (Capable Local Government—implementation readiness) require **plans** that **directors** and **council** can **hold** people **accountable** to.

### Individual vs team plans

| Type | Purpose | Owner | Typical scope |
|------|---------|-------|----------------|
| **Individual** | **Your** **own** **commitments** for the **next** **3–6** **months** (e.g. **PMU** **manager**, **Revenue** **officer**). | **One** **person** | **2–6** **actions** from **your** **role**; **links** to **SDBIP**, **directorate** **priorities**, or **AI** **initiatives** you **own**. |
| **Team** | **Directorate** or **cross‑functional** **group** (e.g. **PMU** + **IT** + **Revenue** for **dashboard**). | **Team** **lead** or **champion**; **actions** **assigned** to **members**. | **5–12** **actions**; **dependencies** between **people**; **shared** **milestones**. |

**Use** **individual** when you are **alone** at the **course** or **own** a **clear** **slice**; **use** **team** when you **attend** as a **group** or will **implement** **with** **others**. **Module 12** **template** works for **both**—**team** **plans** add **multiple** **owners** and **dependencies**.

## Action Plan Structure

A **good** **action** **plan** has **at** **least** these **columns**:

| Element | What it means | Example |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| **Action** | **Specific** **task** or **deliverable** ( **SMART** where possible). | "Complete **data** **inventory** for **revenue** **sources**"; "**Pilot** **prompt** **library** with **3** **PMU** **staff**." |
| **Owner** | **Person** **accountable** (name or **role**). | "J. **Dlamini**"; "**CFO** **office**." |
| **Due date** | **When** it will be **done**. | "31 **Mar** 2026"; "**Q1**." |
| **Resources** | **What** is **needed**: **budget**, **tools**, **data** **access**, **approvals**, **support**. | "**Excel**; **billing** **data** **access**"; "**MM** **approval** for **pilot**." |
| **Success metric** | **How** you will **know** it **succeeded**. | "**Inventory** **signed** **off** by **CFO**"; "**3** **staff** **using** **prompts** **weekly**." |
| **Dependencies** (optional) | **What** must **happen** **first** or **who** **else** is **involved**. | "**IT** **data** **access**"; "**Module 11** **roadmap** **approved**." |

**Link** **actions** to **Module 11** **initiatives** (e.g. "**Revenue** **tracker**—**Foundational**") or **P1–P4** so **council** and **MM** see **alignment** with the **roadmap**.

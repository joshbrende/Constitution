## Module 4: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Data inventory:** For one area (e.g. revenue, assets, complaints), can you name the main dataset(s), where they live, and who owns them? If not, what would you need to do to find out?

2. **Data–KPI link:** For one of your SDBIP or performance KPIs, can you trace the number back to a specific system, report, or process? Is that source timely and of acceptable quality? If not, what is the gap?

3. **Quality:** Which dimension of data quality (accuracy, completeness, timeliness, consistency, validity, uniqueness) is the biggest problem in your municipality? Give one example.

4. **Governance:** Is there a clear owner for the data that feeds your most important KPIs? If not, who should it be, and what would need to change?

5. **AI and data:** Which of the AI tasks in this module would be most useful in your role—quality assessment, cataloging, or cleaning/validation? How would you fit it into your work cycle (e.g. before monthly reporting, during audit prep)?

6. **Prioritisation:** If you could improve only two datasets in the next 6 months, which would they be and why? (Link to KPIs, audits, or a planned dashboard.)

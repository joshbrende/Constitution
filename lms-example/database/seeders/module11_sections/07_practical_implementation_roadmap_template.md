## Practical: Implementation Roadmap Template

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Microsoft Excel**.

This template turns the **phased** **approach** (Module 11 theory) into a **visible** **plan**: **initiatives**, **phases**, **timeline**, **owners**, and **dependencies**. It supports **council** and **management** **briefings** and **Module 12** (Action Planning). **AI** can help **suggest** **initiatives** or **sequencing**—you **populate** with **your** **priorities**; **validate** with **MM** and **IT**.

### Step 1: Create the workbook and sheets

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel**, new workbook.
2. Create sheets: **Roadmap**, **Initiatives list**, and **Cover**.
3. **Cover:** Title "AI Implementation Roadmap — [Municipality]", "As at [date]", "Owner: [e.g. MM office or IT]", "Next review: [date]".

### Step 2: Initiatives list sheet — structure

In the **Initiatives list** sheet:

- **Row 1:** Headers: `ID` | `Initiative` | `Phase` (Quick win / Foundational / Advanced) | `Source (Module)` | `Owner` | `Dependencies` | `Est. start` | `Est. end` | `Status` | `Note`.
- **Rows 2–25:** One row per **initiative**, drawn from **Modules 2–10**, e.g.:
  - *Prompt library and drafting (Module 2)* — Quick win
  - *Indicator extraction and linkage (Module 3)* — Quick win
  - *Data inventory and KPI mapping (Module 4)* — Foundational
  - *One-page dashboard (Module 5)* — Foundational
  - *Revenue and debt tracker (Module 6)* — Foundational
  - *Variance worksheet and AI narrative (Module 6)* — Quick win / Foundational
  - *Faults tracker (Module 7)* — Foundational
  - *Citizen feedback dashboard (Module 7)* — Foundational
  - *Compliance and risk trackers (Module 8)* — Foundational
  - *Demand and scenario worksheets (Module 9)* — Foundational
  - *Engagement tracker and IDP checklist (Module 10)* — Foundational
  - *Chatbot pilot (Module 10)* — Advanced (or Foundational if simple)
  - *Predictive revenue or fault model (Modules 6–7)* — Advanced
  - *Complaint auto-categorisation (Module 10)* — Advanced
  - *Other (add)*

- **Dependencies:** e.g. "Data mapping (M4)"; "Fault system access." **Status:** Not started, **In progress**, **Done**, **Deferred**. **Note:** One line.

### Step 3: Roadmap sheet — timeline view

In the **Roadmap** sheet:

- **Rows** = **Initiatives** (or **grouped** by **phase**). **Columns** = **Months** or **Quarters** (e.g. Q1, Q2, … Q8). **Cells** = **Planned** (e.g. "Start", "Pilot", "Live") or **blank**. **Visual**: **Gantt**-style **bars** or **X** in **range** of **start–end**. **Link** to **Initiatives list** (e.g. **ID** or **name**).
- **Alternatively:** **Table** with **Initiative** | **Phase** | **Q1** | **Q2** | … | **Owner**. **Simple** **text** ("Pilot", "Live") or **colours** (Green = done, **Amber** = in progress, **Grey** = planned).

### Step 4: Summary for council or MM

- **Bottom** of **Roadmap** or **new** **Summary** **sheet:** **3–5** **bullets**: (1) **Phase** **1** (Quick wins) **by** when; (2) **Phase** **2** (Foundational) **key** **deliverables** and **date**; (3) **Phase** **3** (Advanced) **if** **applicable**; (4) **Key** **dependencies** or **risks**; (5) **Resource** **need** (budget, **skills**) **in** **broad** **terms**. **AI** can help **draft** this **from** **your** **roadmap** **summary**—you **edit** and **approve**.

### Step 5: Review cycle

- **Review** **quarterly** or **at** **phase** **gates**. **Update** **Status** and **timeline**; **add** or **defer** **initiatives** as **learning** and **capacity** **allow**. **Module 12** (Action Planning) will **refine** **individual** or **team** **actions** from this **roadmap**.

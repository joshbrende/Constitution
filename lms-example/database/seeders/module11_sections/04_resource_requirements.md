[STEP] Budget — what to cost

## Resource Requirements

**AI** **implementation** requires **budget**, **skills**, and **technology**. **Realistic** **estimates** help **council** and **Treasury** **approve**; **underestimating** leads to **stalled** or **abandoned** **projects**. **SALGA Outcome 1** and **Outcome 2** support **sustainable** implementation—which **includes** **adequate** **resourcing**.

### Budget (examples)

| Category | What to cost | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| **Licences and subscriptions** | **ChatGPT**, **Claude**, **Office 365** **Copilot**, **BI** (Power BI, **Tableau**), **vendor** **AI** **tools**. | **Quick** **wins**: low (e.g. **free** tiers, **existing** **BI**). **Advanced**: **enterprise** **licences**, **API** **costs**. |
| **Vendor and implementation** | **Consultants**, **system** **integrators**, **vendor** **implementation** (e.g. **chatbot**, **predictive** **platform**). | **Scope** **clearly**; **contract** **on** **outcomes** and **support**. |
| **Internal capacity** | **Time** of **staff** (PMU, **Revenue**, **IT**) for **pilots**, **ownership**, **training**. | **Opportunity** **cost**; often **overlooked**. **Part-time** **AI** **lead** or **champion** **allowance**. |
| **Training and change** | **Courses**, **facilitation**, **materials**, **refreshers**. | This **course**; **internal** **workshops**; **external** **vendor** **training** if **needed**. |
| **Data and infrastructure** | **Data** **cleaning**, **integration** **projects**; **cloud** or **server** if **hosting** **models** or **APIs**. | **Foundational** and **advanced**; **link** to **IT** **plan**. |

[STEP] Skills — build, buy, partner

## Skills: Build, Buy, Partner

| Option | What it means | When it fits |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| **Build** | **Train** **existing** staff (this **course**; **prompt** **library**; **Excel**/ **Sheets**; **BI**). | **Quick** **wins**; **foundational** **trackers** and **dashboards**; **sustainable** **ownership**. |
| **Buy** | **Hire** **data** **analysts**, **AI**/ **ML** **specialists**. | **Advanced** **predictive** or **integrated** **systems**; **if** **budget** and **market** **allow**. **Often** **not** **necessary** for **municipal** **quick** **wins** and **foundational** work. |
| **Partner** | **Vendors**, **universities**, **SALGA** or **CoGTA** **programmes**, **other** **municipalities**. | **Advanced** **projects**; **capacity** **building**; **shared** **learning**. |

**Recommendation:** **Build** **first** (train **champions** and **users**); **partner** for **complex** or **niche** (e.g. **predictive** **vendor**); **buy** only when **clearly** **needed** and **funded**.

[STEP] Technology — data, systems, security

## Technology: Data, Systems, Security

- **Data:** **Quality** and **availability** (Module 4). **Budget** for **cleaning**, **integration**, **cataloguing** where **gaps** **block** **use** **cases**.
- **Systems:** **BI** **tools** (often **already** **have**); **APIs** or **connectors** for **automation**; **chatbot** **or** **NLP** **platform** for **advanced**. **Align** with **IT** **strategy** and **security**.
- **Security and POPI:** **Access** **control**; **no** **confidential** or **personal** **data** in **public** **AI**; **vendor** **contracts** must **address** **data** **location**, **ownership**, **and** **POPI**. **AI** can help **draft** **security** or **data** **checklists**—**IT** and **Legal** **validate**.

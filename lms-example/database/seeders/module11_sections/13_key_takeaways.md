## Module 11: Key Takeaways

1. **Change management for AI adoption:** **Resistance** (fear of **replacement**, **distrust**, **skill** **anxiety**, **workload**, **silo**) must be **addressed** through **reframing**, **pilots**, **training**, and **communication**. **Champions** and **early** **adopters** **model** use; **training** includes **ethics** and **POPI**; **communication** from **MM** and **directors** is **consistent** and **repeated**. **AI** can **draft** **comms** **or** **FAQs**—you **customise** and **approve**.

2. **Phased implementation:** **Quick** **wins** (0–6 m): **prompts**, **checklists**, **drafting**, **specs**—low **cost**, **build** **trust**. **Foundational** (6–18 m): **data** **mapping**, **trackers**, **dashboards**, **policies**. **Advanced** (18–36 m): **integrated** **systems**, **predictive**, **vendor** **solutions**. **Sequence** by **dependencies** (data, **skills**, **integration**). **Roadmap** **template** (Practical) **maps** **initiatives** to **phases** and **timeline**.

3. **Resource requirements:** **Budget**: **licences**, **vendor**, **internal** **time**, **training**, **data**/ **infrastructure**. **Skills**: **build** (train) **first**; **partner** for **complex**; **buy** when **necessary**. **Technology**: **data** **quality**, **systems**, **security** and **POPI**. **Realistic** **estimates** support **council** and **Treasury** **approval**.

4. **Vendor selection and partnerships:** **Criteria**: **fit**, **cost**, **data**/ **security**, **support**, **integration**. **Evaluation**: **shortlist**, **demo**, **pilot**, **references**; **procurement** **rules** **apply**. **Contracts**: **scope**, **SLA**, **data** **ownership**, **POPI**, **termination**. **Partnerships**: **SALGA**, **CoGTA**, **other** **municipalities** for **capacity** and **shared** **learning**.

5. **Success metrics and evaluation:** **Adoption** (who **uses**); **efficiency** (time/ **cost** **saved**); **quality** (errors, **SLA**); **outcome** ( **SALGA**, **audit**). **When**: **ongoing**, **milestone**, **annual**. **Baseline** **before** **start**; **review** and **adapt**; **update** **roadmap**. **AI** can **suggest** **evaluation** **frameworks**—you **run** and **own**.

6. **Practical skills:** You can **build** an **implementation** **roadmap** **template** ( **initiatives** **list**, **phases**, **timeline**, **owners**, **dependencies**) and an **AI** **initiative** **prioritisation** **matrix** ( **criteria**, **scores**, **rank**, **P1–P4**). Both support **council** **briefings** and **Module 12** (Action Planning).

7. **AI Application—data and validation:** Use **generic** or **aggregated** **inputs** only; **never** **confidential** **budget**, **contract**, or **vendor** **detail** in public AI. **AI** suggests **maturity** **frameworks**, **planning** **templates**, **ROI**/ **risk** **structures**, **success** **factors**—you **populate**, **validate** with **MM**, **CFO**, **IT**, or **Risk**, and **own** the **final** **roadmap**, **business** **case**, and **governance**.

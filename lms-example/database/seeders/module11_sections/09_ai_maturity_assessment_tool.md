## AI Application: AI Maturity Assessment

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

An **AI** **maturity** **assessment** helps you **understand** **where** your municipality **is** (e.g. **awareness**, **experimenting**, **piloting**, **scaling**) across **dimensions** such as **strategy**, **data**, **skills**, **use** **cases**, **governance**, and **technology**. **AI** can: (1) **suggest** **maturity** **levels** and **descriptions**; (2) **generate** **assessment** **questions** for **each** **dimension**; (3) **draft** a **short** **report** **structure** (e.g. "Dimension X: Level 2—because…"). You **run** the **assessment** with **your** **team**; **use** **generic** or **aggregated** **answers** if you **paste** into **AI**—**never** **confidential** **strategy** or **budget** **detail**.

[STEP] Task A — Suggest maturity levels and dimensions

### Task A: Suggest Maturity Levels and Dimensions

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We are a South African municipality. We want an **AI** **maturity** **assessment** **framework**. Suggest: (1) **4–5** **maturity** **levels** (e.g. 1=Awareness, 2=Experimenting, 3=Piloting, 4=Scaling, 5=Optimising) with a **one-line** **description** each; (2) **5–6** **dimensions** to **assess** (e.g. **Strategy** and **leadership**, **Data** **readiness**, **Skills** and **capacity**, **Use** **cases** and **adoption**, **Governance** and **ethics**, **Technology** and **tools**). For each **dimension**, give a **one-line** description. Use **tables**. We will use this to **design** our **own** **assessment**—we are NOT putting confidential data into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Adapt** **levels** and **dimensions** to your **context**. Use as **structure** for **internal** **workshop** or **survey**. **AI** suggests; **you** **own** the **framework**.

[STEP] Task B — Generate assessment questions per dimension

### Task B: Generate Assessment Questions per Dimension

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > For a municipal **AI** **maturity** **assessment**, we have **dimensions**: Strategy and leadership; Data readiness; Skills and capacity; Use cases and adoption; Governance and ethics; Technology and tools. For **each** **dimension**, suggest **3–4** **yes/no** or **multiple-choice** **questions** that help **determine** maturity **level** (1–5). For each question, indicate what **answer** would suggest **level** 1 vs 3 vs 5. Use a **table** or **bullets**. We will **run** the **assessment** ourselves—we are NOT putting our answers into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Refine** with **MM** or **steering**. Use in **workshop** (e.g. **raised** **hands**, **dot** **voting**) or **short** **survey**. **Score** each **dimension**; **average** or **report** **per** **dimension**. **AI** suggests **questions**; **you** **run** and **interpret**.

[STEP] Task C — Draft a maturity summary from self-assessed levels

### Task C: Draft a Maturity Summary from Self-Assessed Levels

1. In a **new chat**, provide **aggregated** **results** only. Example:

   > Our AI maturity self-assessment: Strategy 2 (Experimenting); Data 2; Skills 2; Use cases 2; Governance 1 (Awareness); Technology 2. Overall we are at **level 2**. Our **strengths**: some **data** **mapping** and **prompt** use. **Gaps**: **governance** (policy, **POPI**); **scaling** **use** **cases**.

2. Then send:

   > Using only the information above, write a **one-page** **maturity** **summary** for the Municipal Manager. Include: (1) **overall** **level** and **one** **sentence** what it means; (2) **strengths** (2–3 bullets); (3) **gaps** (2–3 bullets); (4) **three** **recommended** **next** **steps** to **advance** maturity. Formal language. Do not add. We will verify and approve.

2. **Use the output:** **Edit** and **approve** with **MM**. Use in **roadmap** **or** **council** **briefing**. **AI** drafts from **your** **self-assessment**; **you** **own** the **content**.

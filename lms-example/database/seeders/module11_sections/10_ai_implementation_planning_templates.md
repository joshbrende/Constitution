## AI Application: Implementation Planning Templates

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**Implementation** **planning** **templates** give you a **standard** **structure** for **scoping**, **scheduling**, and **managing** AI **initiatives**. **AI** can: (1) **suggest** a **pilot** **plan** **template** (objectives, **scope**, **timeline**, **team**, **success** **criteria**, **risks**); (2) **generate** a **phase** **gate** **checklist** (what to **review** before **moving** to **next** **phase**); (3) **draft** a **stakeholder** **communication** **plan** (who, **when**, **what** **message**). You **customise** and **populate** with **your** **initiative**; **use** **generic** **descriptions** in **AI**—**never** **confidential** **detail**.

[STEP] Task A — Generate a pilot plan template

### Task A: Generate a Pilot Plan Template

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We are a South African municipality. We want a **pilot** **plan** **template** for an **AI** **initiative** (e.g. **prompt** **library**, **dashboard**, **chatbot**). The template should include: (1) **Objectives** (2–3); (2) **Scope** (in / out); (3) **Timeline** (e.g. **weeks** 1–4: **prep**; 5–8: **pilot**; 9–10: **review**); (4) **Team** (roles: **owner**, **champion**, **IT** **support**, **users**); (5) **Success** **criteria** (3–4); (6) **Risks** and **mitigations** (2–3). Use **headings** and **bullets**. We will **fill** in for our **own** **pilot**—we are NOT putting our data into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Adopt** as **template** for **each** **pilot** (e.g. **Module 12** **action** **plan** **per** **initiative**). **AI** suggests **structure**; **you** **populate** and **own**.

[STEP] Task B — Suggest a phase gate checklist

### Task B: Suggest a Phase Gate Checklist

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > For **AI** **implementation** in a municipality, we want **phase** **gate** **checklists**: **questions** or **criteria** to **review** before **moving** from (1) **Quick** **win** to **Foundational**; (2) **Foundational** to **Advanced**. For each **gate**, suggest **5–7** **yes/no** or **short** **criteria** (e.g. "Data for next phase is mapped and owned"; "Champions are active in at least 2 directorates"; "Governance or AI use policy is drafted"). We will use this in our **steering** **meetings**—we are NOT putting our answers into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Customise** for your **phases** and **governance**. Use in **quarterly** or **phase** **reviews**. **AI** suggests; **you** **adopt** and **run**.

[STEP] Task C — Draft a stakeholder communication plan structure

### Task C: Draft a Stakeholder Communication Plan Structure

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > We are rolling out **AI** **implementation** in our municipality. We need a **stakeholder** **communication** **plan**: **who** to **inform** or **engage** (e.g. **MM**, **directors**, **council**, **staff**, **unions**), **when** (e.g. **before** **pilot**, **at** **launch**, **quarterly**), and **what** **message** or **channel** (e.g. **all-staff** **email**, **council** **report**, **workshop**). Suggest a **table** with **Stakeholder** | **When** | **Message** (one line) | **Channel**. We will **customise**—we are NOT putting our internal comms into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Fill** in **your** **stakeholders** and **messages**. **Communications** or **MM** **office** **approves**. **AI** suggests **structure**; **you** **own** the **content** and **execution**.

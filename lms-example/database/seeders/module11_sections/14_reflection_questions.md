## Module 11: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Change management:** Who could **champion** AI in your municipality ( **MM**, **director**, **PMU**, **Revenue**)? What **resistance** do you **anticipate**—and **one** **action** to **address** it? Would **AI**-drafted **comms** or **FAQs** help—and who **approves**?

2. **Phasing:** Which **quick** **wins** (from **Modules 2–6**) could you **start** in the **next** **3** **months** with **minimal** **budget**? What **foundational** **work** ( **data** **mapping**, **trackers**) is **blocking** **other** **initiatives**—and who **owns** it?

3. **Resources:** What **budget** (if any) do you have or could you **request** for **AI** ( **licences**, **training**, **vendor**)? **Skills**: **build**, **buy**, or **partner**—which **fits** your **context**? **Technology**: **data** **gaps** or **IT** **constraints**?

4. **Vendors:** Are you **considering** a **vendor** for **chatbot**, **predictive**, or **BI**? What **criteria** would you **use** to **evaluate**—and does your **procurement** **process** **support** **AI** **contracts** ( **data**, **SLA**, **POPI**)?

5. **Success and evaluation:** What **would** you **measure** to **show** AI **adoption** and **impact** (e.g. **staff** **trained**, **hours** **saved**, **dashboards** **live**)? **Who** **reviews** and **how** **often**? Would **AI**-suggested **maturity** or **evaluation** **frameworks** help—and who **validates**?

6. **Roadmap and prioritisation:** Have you **drafted** a **roadmap** or **prioritisation** (from the **Practicals**)? What are your **top** **3** **P1** **initiatives** for the **next** **12** **months**—and what **depends** on **them** ( **data**, **champion**, **budget**)?

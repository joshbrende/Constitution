## AI Application: ROI Calculator and Risk Assessment

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**ROI** (return on investment) and **risk** **assessment** help **justify** and **govern** AI **spend**. **AI** can: (1) **suggest** an **ROI** **calculator** **structure** ( **costs** vs **benefits**; **payback**; **assumptions**); (2) **propose** a **risk** **assessment** **template** for **AI** **implementation** ( **risks**, **likelihood**, **impact**, **mitigations**); (3) **draft** a **one-page** **business** **case** **outline** from **generic** **inputs** you provide. **You** **populate** with **your** **numbers**; **never** put **confidential** **budget** or **vendor** **prices** into **public** **AI**. **Validate** with **CFO** and **Risk**.

[STEP] Task A — Suggest an ROI calculator structure

### Task A: Suggest an ROI Calculator Structure

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We are a South African municipality. We want an **ROI** **calculator** **structure** for **AI** **initiatives**. We will NOT put our figures into AI. Suggest: (1) **Cost** **categories** (e.g. **licences**, **implementation**, **internal** **time**, **training**) with **one** **line** each; (2) **Benefit** **categories** (e.g. **time** **saved** (hours × rate), **error** **reduction**, **faster** **resolution**, **revenue** **or** **collection** **impact**) with **one** **line** each; (3) **formula** in **words** (e.g. **ROI** = (Total benefits − Total costs) / Total costs × 100; **Payback** = Costs / Annual benefits in years). Use **headings** and **bullets**. We will **build** in Excel with our own numbers.

2. **Use the output:** **Build** **Excel** **template**. **Populate** for **one** or **two** **initiatives** (e.g. **prompt** **library** = low cost, **estimate** **hours** **saved**; **chatbot** = **licence** + **implementation**, **estimate** **call** **centre** **reduction**). **CFO** **reviews** **assumptions**. **AI** suggests **structure**; **you** **own** the **model** and **figures**.

[STEP] Task B — Propose a risk assessment for AI implementation

### Task B: Propose a Risk Assessment for AI Implementation

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > We are implementing **AI** in our municipality. We want a **risk** **assessment** **template** for **AI** **implementation**: **risks** that are **specific** to **AI** (e.g. **data** **quality**, **POPI**, **over-reliance** on **AI**, **vendor** **lock-in**, **skill** **gap**, **resistance**, **accuracy** of **outputs**). For each: (1) **Risk**; (2) **Likelihood** (1–5 or Low/Med/High); (3) **Impact** (1–5 or Low/Med/High); (4) **Mitigation** (one line). Use a **table**. We will **score** and **add** **mitigations** ourselves—we are NOT putting confidential risk detail into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Score** with **Risk** or **steering** **committee**. **Add** to **corporate** **risk** **register** or **AI** **governance** **doc**. **AI** suggests **risks**; **you** **own** the **assessment** and **actions**.

[STEP] Task C — Draft a business case outline

### Task C: Draft a Business Case Outline

1. In a **new chat**, provide **generic** or **aggregated** **inputs** only. Example:

   > We want to **pilot** a **chatbot** for **citizen** **queries**. Generic: **cost** **estimate** **R** X **for** **licence** and **setup**; **benefit** **estimate** **Y** **hours** **saved** per **month** in **call** **centre** at **rate** Z. **Risks**: **data** **quality**; **user** **adoption**. **Strategic** **fit**: **SALGA** **Outcome** 4 and 5.

2. Then send:

   > Using only the information above, write a **one-page** **business** **case** **outline** for **council** or **MM**: (1) **Initiative** and **objectives**; (2) **Costs** and **benefits** (summary); (3) **ROI** or **payback** in **words**; (4) **Risks** and **mitigations**; (5) **Ask** (approval, **budget**). Use **placeholders** like [X], [Y] where we will add our numbers. Formal language. Do not add. We will verify and approve.

2. **Use the output:** **Replace** **placeholders** with **your** **figures**. **CFO** and **MM** **approve** before **council**. **AI** drafts **structure**; **you** **own** the **content** and **numbers**.

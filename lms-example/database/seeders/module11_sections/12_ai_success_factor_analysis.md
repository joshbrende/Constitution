## AI Application: Success Factor Analysis

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**Success** **factors** are **conditions** or **actions** that **increase** the **likelihood** that **AI** **implementation** **succeeds**. **AI** can: (1) **suggest** **success** **factors** for **municipal** AI **adoption** (e.g. **leadership**, **champions**, **data**, **governance**, **training**); (2) **propose** a **checklist** you can **use** **before** **launching** a **pilot** or **phase**; (3) **draft** a **short** "**readiness**" or "**lessons** **learned**" **summary** from **aggregated** **inputs** you provide. You **validate** with **MM** or **steering**; **use** **generic** **inputs** in **AI**—**never** **confidential** **evaluations**.

[STEP] Task A — Suggest success factors for municipal AI adoption

### Task A: Suggest Success Factors for Municipal AI Adoption

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > We are a South African municipality implementing **AI**. We want **success** **factors**: **conditions** or **actions** that **make** AI **adoption** more **likely** to **succeed**. Suggest **10–12** **factors** in **categories**: (1) **Leadership** and **governance**; (2) **People** and **skills**; (3) **Data** and **technology**; (4) **Use** **cases** and **change** **management**. For each **factor**, give: **one** **sentence** on **why** it **matters**; **one** **example** of what "**good**" looks like. Use a **table**. We will use this as a **checklist** and **discussion** **guide**—we are NOT putting our data into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Discuss** in **steering** or **planning** **workshop**. **Tick** which you **have**; **plan** to **address** **gaps**. **AI** suggests; **you** **own** the **checklist** and **actions**.

[STEP] Task B — Propose a "ready to launch" pilot checklist

### Task B: Propose a "Ready to Launch" Pilot Checklist

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > Before **launching** an **AI** **pilot** (e.g. **prompt** **library**, **dashboard**, **chatbot**) in a municipality, what **should** we **check**? Give **10–12** **yes/no** or **short** **criteria** in **categories**: **Scope** and **objectives**; **Team** and **owner**; **Data** and **access**; **Training** and **support**; **Governance** (e.g. **human** **sign-off**, **POPI**); **Success** **criteria** and **review** **date**. We will use this **before** **each** **pilot**—we are NOT putting our data into AI.

2. **Use the output:** **Adopt** as **pre-launch** **checklist**. **Owner** and **steering** **sign** **off** before **pilot** **goes** **live**. **AI** suggests; **you** **run** the **check**.

[STEP] Task C — Draft a readiness or lessons-learned summary

### Task C: Draft a Readiness or Lessons-Learned Summary

1. In a **new chat**, provide **aggregated** **inputs** only. Example:

   > We **assessed** our **readiness** for **AI** **phase** 2: **Leadership** strong; **data** **mapping** done for 2 areas; **champions** in 3 directorates; **governance** **policy** still **draft**. **Lessons** from **phase** 1: **training** **critical**; **quick** **wins** **built** **trust**. **Gap**: **POPI** **checklist** for **AI** **use** not yet **finalised**.

2. Then send:

   > Using only the information above, write a **one-paragraph** **readiness** or **lessons-learned** **summary** for the Municipal Manager. Include: **readiness** **in** **one** **sentence**; **one** **strength**; **one** **gap** to **address** **before** **phase** 2; **one** **lesson** from **phase** 1. Formal language. Do not add. We will verify and approve.

2. **Use the output:** **Edit** and **approve** with **MM**. Use in **steering** or **roadmap** **review**. **AI** drafts from **your** **summary**; **you** **own** the **content**.

## When to Use Which AI Task

| Module 11 area | AI task | What you get | What you must do |
|----------------|---------|--------------|------------------|
| **Maturity** | Levels, dimensions, questions; maturity summary | Framework and narrative | Run **assessment** with your team; use **generic** or **aggregated** in AI. |
| **Planning** | Pilot plan; phase gate; comms plan | Templates and structures | **Customise** and **populate**; **never** **confidential** **detail** in public AI. |
| **ROI and risk** | ROI structure; risk table; business case outline | Structures and draft | **Build** **Excel** **ROI**; **score** **risks**; **add** **your** **numbers**; **CFO**/ **Risk** **validate**. |
| **Success factors** | Success factors; pilot checklist; readiness summary | Checklists and narrative | **Adopt** **checklists**; **run** **pre-launch**; **validate** **summary** with **MM**. |

**Remember:** **AI** **supports** **structure**, **templates**, and **narrative**—it does **not** replace **your** **roadmap**, **budget**, or **governance** **decisions**. Use **generic** or **aggregated** **inputs** only; **validate** with **MM**, **CFO**, **IT**, or **Risk** before use in **council** or **Treasury**.

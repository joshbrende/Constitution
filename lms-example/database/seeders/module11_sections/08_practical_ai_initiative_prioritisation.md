## Practical: AI Initiative Prioritisation

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Microsoft Excel**.

When **resources** and **time** are **limited**, you must **prioritise** **which** AI **initiatives** to **do** **first**. This **matrix** uses **criteria** (e.g. **value**, **feasibility**, **strategic** **fit**) to **score** and **rank** **initiatives** from your **roadmap**. **AI** can help **suggest** **criteria** and **weights**—you **score** and **decide**; **validate** with **MM** or **steering** **committee**.

### Step 1: Define criteria and weights

1. **Criteria** (examples):
   - **Value** (1–5): **Impact** on **efficiency**, **quality**, or **outcomes** (e.g. **SALGA** **alignment**, **revenue**, **compliance**).
   - **Feasibility** (1–5): **Ease** of **implementation**—**data** **available**, **skills** **present**, **low** **integration** **effort**.
   - **Strategic fit** (1–5): **Alignment** with **IDP**, **SDBIP**, **council** **priorities**, or **audit**/ **risk** **gaps**.
   - **Quick win** (1–5): **Can** **deliver** **visible** **result** in **&lt;3** **months**?

2. **Weights** (optional): e.g. **Value** 30%, **Feasibility** 25%, **Strategic** 25%, **Quick win** 20%. **Or** **equal** **weight**. **Document** so **scores** are **transparent**.

### Step 2: Score initiatives

- **Rows** = **Initiatives** (from **Roadmap** **Initiatives** **list**). **Columns** = **Value** | **Feasibility** | **Strategic** | **Quick win** | **Weighted** **score** (or **sum** if **equal** **weight**) | **Rank**.
- **Score** each **1–5** (1 = low, 5 = high). **Weighted** **score** = **sum** of (weight × score). **Rank** = **sort** **descending** by **score**.
- **Use** **this** to **order** or **phase** **initiatives**: **top** **ranked** = **do** **first** (subject to **dependencies**).

### Step 3: Quadrant or matrix view (optional)

- **Two** **axes**: e.g. **Value** (X) vs **Feasibility** (Y). **Plot** each **initiative**. **Top-right** (high value, high feasibility) = **prioritise**. **Bottom-left** = **defer** or **revisit**. **Top-left** (high value, low feasibility) = **invest** in **feasibility** (e.g. **data**, **skills**) **first**.
- **Simple** **version**: **Table** with **Priority** **column**: **P1** (do first), **P2** (next), **P3** (when **capacity** **allows**), **P4** (defer). **Assign** **P1–P4** **from** **rank** and **dependencies**.

### Step 4: One-sentence "Recommended order"

- **Bottom** of **sheet**: *"Recommended order for next 12 months:"* e.g. "P1: Prompt library, data mapping, revenue tracker; P2: Faults and compliance trackers, variance narrative; P3: Chatbot pilot. P4: Predictive models." **Share** with **MM** or **steering** for **approval**. **Module 12** will **turn** **P1** into **concrete** **actions** and **owners**.

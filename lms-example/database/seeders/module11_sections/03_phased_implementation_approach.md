[STEP] Why phase AI implementation?

## Phased Implementation Approach

**Phasing** helps you: (1) **build** **momentum** with **quick** **wins**; (2) **establish** **foundations** (data, **governance**, **skills**) before **complex** **integrations**; (3) **manage** **risk** and **resource** **constraint**; (4) **learn** and **adapt** before **scaling**. **SALGA Outcome 1** (Agile Force) and **Outcome 2** (Customised Innovative Solutions—practical roadmap) support **incremental**, **sustainable** adoption.

### Three phases (conceptual)

| Phase | Focus | Examples (from Modules 2–10) | Typical horizon |
|-------|-------|------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Quick wins** | **Low** **cost**, **low** **integration**; **immediate** **value**; **build** **trust**. | **Prompts** for **drafting**, **summarising**, **checklists** (Module 2); **indicator** **extraction**, **linkage** **tables** (Module 3); **data** **quality** **and** **cataloguing** (Module 4); **dashboard** **specs**, **anomaly** **rules**, **NLG** **insights** (Module 5); **variance** **narratives**, **debt** **rules** (Module 6). | **0–6** **months** |
| **Foundational** | **Data** **readiness**; **trackers** and **dashboards** **in** **use**; **policies** (e.g. **AI** **use**, **POPI**); **first** **integrated** **tools** (e.g. **BI** **with** **alerts**). | **Revenue**/ **debt** **tracker**; **variance** **worksheet**; **faults** **tracker**; **citizen** **feedback** **dashboard**; **compliance** and **risk** **trackers** (Modules 6–8); **demand** and **scenario** **worksheets** (Module 9). **Chatbot** **pilot** (Module 10) if **platform** **ready**. | **6–18** **months** |
| **Advanced** | **Integrated** **systems**; **predictive** **models** **in** **production**; **vendor** **solutions**; **automation** of **routing**, **categorisation**, **forecasting**. | **In-house** or **vendor** **predictive** (revenue, **faults**, **satisfaction**); **complaint** **auto-categorisation** and **routing**; **NLP** **pipelines** for **submissions**; **lifecycle** and **climate** **models** (Modules 6–10). | **18–36** **months** |

[STEP] Sequencing and dependencies

## Sequencing and Dependencies

- **Dependencies:** **Quick** **wins** often need **only** **access** to **ChatGPT**/ **Claude** (or **similar**) and **training**. **Foundational** needs **data** **quality** and **ownership** (Module 4); **trackers** **need** **data** **sources**. **Advanced** needs **integration** with **billing**, **fault**, or **complaint** **systems**; **vendor** or **IT** **capacity**.
- **Sequencing:** **Start** with **quick** **wins** that **don't** **depend** on **clean** **data** or **new** **systems** (e.g. **drafting**, **checklists**). **In parallel**, **improve** **data** and **trackers** so **foundational** **dashboards** and **alerts** can **go** **live**. **Advanced** **follows** when **foundations** and **budget** are **in** **place**.
- **Your roadmap (Practical):** You will **map** **initiatives** to **phases** and **timeline**; **AI** can help **suggest** **sequencing** from **generic** **descriptions**—you **validate** with **MM** and **IT**.

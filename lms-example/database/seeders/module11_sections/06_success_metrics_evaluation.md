[STEP] Why measure AI implementation success?

## Success Metrics and Evaluation

**Measuring** **success** helps you: (1) **justify** **investment** to **council** and **Treasury**; (2) **learn** what **works** and what **doesn't**; (3) **adapt** and **improve**; (4) **sustain** **momentum**. **SALGA Outcome 1** (Agile Force) and **Outcome 2** (Customised Innovative Solutions—practical roadmap) benefit from **evidence** of **progress** and **impact**.

### What to measure (examples)

| Level | What to measure | Examples |
|-------|-----------------|----------|
| **Adoption** | **Are** people **using** AI? | **Number** of **staff** **trained**; **number** **using** **prompts** or **tools** **monthly**; **champions** **active**; **use** **cases** **live**. |
| **Efficiency** | **Time** or **cost** **saved**? | **Hours** **saved** per **month** (e.g. **drafting**, **report** **prep**); **reduction** in **manual** **data** **entry** or **rework**; **faster** **turnaround** (e.g. **complaint** **routing**). |
| **Quality** | **Better** **outputs**? | **Fewer** **errors** (e.g. **variance** **narrative** **accuracy**); **higher** **resolution** **rate** or **SLA** **compliance**; **improved** **satisfaction** (if **linkable** to AI **intervention**). |
| **Outcome** | **Impact** on **strategic** **goals**? | **SALGA** **outcome** **alignment** (e.g. **collection** **rate**, **compliance**, **engagement**); **audit** **or** **oversight** **improvement**. **Attribution** to AI can be **hard**—use **contribution** rather than **claim** **sole** **cause**. |

[STEP] When and how to evaluate

## When and How to Evaluate

- **When:** **Ongoing** (e.g. **quarterly** **adoption** **and** **efficiency** **counts**); **milestone** (e.g. **after** **pilot**, **after** **phase** **1**); **annual** (e.g. **roadmap** **review**, **budget** **cycle**). **Baseline** **before** **starting** (e.g. **hours** **per** **report**, **resolution** **rate**) so you can **compare**.
- **How:** **Surveys** (e.g. "Have you used AI for X in the last month?"); **system** **logs** (if **available**); **manager** **estimation** (e.g. **time** **saved**); **KPI** **dashboards** (Modules 5–8). **Keep** **simple**; **avoid** **over-measurement** that **burdens** **staff**.
- **Learning and adaptation:** **Review** **metrics** in **steering** or **AI** **governance** **meetings**. **What** **worked**? **What** **didn't**? **Pivot** (e.g. **drop** **low-adoption** **use** **case**; **double** **down** on **high-value** one). **Update** **roadmap** and **prioritisation** (Module 12) **accordingly**. **AI** can help **draft** **evaluation** **frameworks** or **review** **agendas**—you **validate** and **run** the **process**.

[STEP] When to use vendors

## Vendor Selection and Partnerships

**Vendors** and **partners** can provide: **platforms** (chatbot, **BI**, **predictive**); **implementation** and **integration**; **training**; **ongoing** **support**. **When** to **use** them: (1) **in-house** **capacity** or **skills** are **insufficient**; (2) **specialised** **technology** (e.g. **ML** **models**, **NLP** **pipelines**) is **needed**; (3) **faster** **time-to-value** than **building** **from** **scratch**. **SALGA Outcome 2** (Customised Innovative Solutions) supports **practical** **solutions**—**vendor** or **partner** when they **fit** **better** than **DIY**.

### Selection criteria (examples)

| Criterion | What to assess |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Fit for use case** | Does the **product** or **service** **address** your **need** (e.g. **complaint** **routing**, **revenue** **prediction**, **chatbot**)? **Municipal** or **government** **experience**? |
| **Total cost of ownership** | **Licence** **and** **implementation**; **ongoing** **support**; **internal** **effort** to **run** and **maintain**. **Hidden** **costs** (data **prep**, **integration**). |
| **Data and security** | **Where** is **data** **processed** (on-prem, **cloud**, **jurisdiction**)? **POPI** **compliance**; **contract** **terms** on **data** **ownership** and **deletion**. |
| **Vendor stability and support** | **Track** **record**; **references**; **support** **model** (SLA, **helpdesk**). **Exit** **strategy** if **vendor** **fails** or **contract** **ends**. |
| **Integration** | **Works** with **your** **billing**, **fault**, **complaint**, or **BI** **systems**? **APIs**, **export** **formats**. |

[STEP] Evaluation and contracts

## Evaluation and Contracts

- **Evaluation:** **Shortlist** (2–3) from **RFI** or **market** **scan**; **demo** or **pilot** on **your** **data** (anonymised or **synthetic** if **sensitive**); **score** against **criteria**; **reference** **checks**. **Procurement** and **SCM** **rules** (MFMA, **municipal** **policy**) **apply**—**follow** **due** **process**.
- **Contracts:** **Scope**; **deliverables**; **timeline**; **price** and **payment**; **SLA**; **data** **ownership**, **security**, **POPI**; **IP**; **termination** and **handover**. **Legal** and **IT** **review**. **AI** can help **suggest** **contract** **clauses** or **checklists**—**Legal** **owns** the **final** **terms**.

## Partnerships: SALGA, CoGTA, Other Municipalities

- **SALGA**, **CoGTA**, **provincial** **programmes:** **Capacity** **building**, **pilots**, **shared** **procurement** or **frameworks**. **Engage** **early** to **align** and **avoid** **duplication**.
- **Other municipalities:** **Peer** **learning**; **shared** **lessons**; **possible** **joint** **tender** for **common** **solutions** (e.g. **complaint** **system**, **BI** **template**). **Networks** from this **course** can **support** **ongoing** **exchange**.

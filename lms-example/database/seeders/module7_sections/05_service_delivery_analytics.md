## Service Delivery Analytics

**Service delivery analytics** is the use of **data** to understand **performance** (outputs and outcomes) for water, sanitation, electricity, roads, waste, and other services. It supports: (1) **SDBIP** and **IDP** reporting; (2) **dashboards** for the MM, directors, and council; (3) **root-cause** analysis when KPIs are off track; (4) **benchmarking** and **improvement** priorities. The same **dashboard** principles from Module 5 apply: purpose, audience, KPIs from your plan, traffic lights, "Data as at."

### KPIs by service area (examples)

| Service | Output KPIs (examples) | Outcome KPIs (examples) | Data source |
|---------|------------------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| **Water** | New connections; faults reported; faults resolved in X days; % of accounts metered. | % with access; water quality compliance; interruptions per month; citizen satisfaction. | Billing; fault system; labs; surveys. |
| **Sanitation** | New connections; blockages cleared; desludging. | % with access; sewage overflow incidents; satisfaction. | Technical; fault; surveys. |
| **Electricity** | Faults; resolution time; new connections. | Interruptions; duration (SAIDI/SAIFI if applicable); satisfaction. | Fault; meter; technical. |
| **Roads** | km resurfaced; potholes repaired; gravel upgraded. | % roads in good/acceptable condition; complaints. | Technical; asset; complaints. |
| **Waste** | Tonnage collected; % households with collection; illegal dumps cleared. | Coverage; satisfaction; recycling rate (if tracked). | Operations; complaints; surveys. |

[STEP] Trends and root-cause questions

## Trends and Root-Cause Questions

- **Trends:** Track KPIs **over time** (monthly, quarterly). Is resolution time improving? Are faults rising in one area? **Charts** (line, bar) in your dashboard help.
- **Root-cause:** When a KPI is **off track**, ask: *Volume* (more faults?)? *Capacity* (not enough crews or parts)? *Asset condition* (ageing infrastructure)? *Process* (logging, prioritisation, handover)? *External* (load-shedding, drought)? **AI** can help **draft** a short "root-cause" or "what to watch" narrative from **aggregated** KPI summaries—you **validate**.

[STEP] Data sources and refresh

## Data Sources and Refresh

- **Operational systems:** Fault logging, work orders, asset registers, billing. **Refresh:** Daily or weekly for **operational** dashboards; monthly for **SDBIP**-style reporting.
- **Surveys and feedback:** Often **quarterly** or **annual** for satisfaction; complaints **continuous**. **AI** can help **categorise** and **summarise** **anonymised** feedback—**batch** (e.g. monthly) is typical.
- **Align with data–KPI mapping (Module 4):** Ensure each KPI has a **defined source**, **owner**, and **refresh**. Gaps (no data, poor quality) should be in your **improvement** plan.

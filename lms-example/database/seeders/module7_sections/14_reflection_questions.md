## Module 7: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Service delivery standards:** Does your **IDP** or **SDBIP** explicitly state **accessibility**, **affordability**, and **quality** standards for water, sanitation, electricity, roads, and waste? Which **one** standard would you add or tighten in the next planning cycle—and do you have the **data** to measure it?

2. **Infrastructure and faults:** Do you have a **faults** or **work-order** system? How do you track **resolution time** and **backlog ageing**? Is there a **target** (e.g. 48h) in your **SDBIP**? Would **prioritisation rules** or a simple **"when to inspect"** logic (from the AI tasks) help—and where would you **implement** it (Excel, your system, both)?

3. **Citizen satisfaction:** How does your municipality measure **satisfaction** (surveys, complaints, both)? Do you report **themes** from feedback? If you used **AI** to **categorise** or **summarise** feedback: have you **anonymised** it (no names, IDs, contact details)? Who would **validate** AI-suggested themes before they go into reports?

4. **Analytics and benchmarking:** Which **service delivery KPIs** are in your **SDBIP** and **dashboard**? Do you **benchmark** (internally over time, vs target, vs other municipalities)? Where do you get **benchmark** data—and would a **"How we compare"** paragraph (AI-drafted from your aggregates) help in the annual report or council briefing?

5. **Practical trackers:** Have you built (or could you build) a **faults and resolution tracker** and a **citizen feedback** summary? What **data** do you have (fault system, complaints, surveys)—and what is **missing**? Who would **own** these trackers and **approve** the traffic-light rules?

6. **AI and POPI:** For the **AI Application** tasks: did you use **only aggregated** or **anonymised** data for citizen feedback? What would you need (e.g. an **anonymisation** checklist, **customer care** sign-off) to make **theme** or **sentiment** analysis with AI **routine** and **POPI-compliant**?

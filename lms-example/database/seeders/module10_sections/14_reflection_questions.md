## Module 10: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Digital platforms:** Which **digital** **channels** does your municipality use for **citizen** **engagement** (website, app, **social**, **online** **consultation**)? Is there a **plan** to **add** (e.g. **chatbot**, **app**)? Who **owns** **content** and **feedback** **analysis**—**Communications**, **Customer Care**, **IT**?

2. **Feedback and complaints:** How does **feedback** from **different** **channels** (complaints, **social**, **surveys**, **IDP**) get **aggregated** and **themed**? Do you **anonymise** before any **AI** or **analysis**? Would **AI**-assisted **sentiment** or **categorisation** (with **anonymisation**) help—and who **validates** **themes**?

3. **Complaint system:** Do you have a **single** **complaint** **log** with **routing**, **SLA**, and **escalation**? Would **routing** **rules** or **auto-categorisation** (from **AI**-suggested **keywords**) help—and where would you **implement** (CRM, **custom**, **vendor**)?

4. **IDP participation:** Are **IDP** **consultation** **steps** (notice, **meetings**, **submissions**, **report** to **council**, **feedback**) **documented** and **tracked**? How do you **summarise** **written** **submissions** for **council**—**manual** or **NLP**? If **NLP**, is **anonymisation** in place and **POPI**-compliant?

5. **Transparency:** Is there a **checklist** of **what** to **publish** (IDP, **budget**, **minutes**, **performance**, **contact**) and **when**? Who **owns** **updates**? Would an **AI**-suggested **checklist** help—and who **validates**?

6. **Chatbots and predictive satisfaction:** Would a **chatbot** for **FAQs** **reduce** **load** on your **call** **centre**—and what **platform** would you use (your **website**, **vendor**)? For **predictive** **satisfaction**: do you **track** **leading** **indicators** (resolution, **sentiment**, **backlog**)—and would **AI**-suggested **indicators** and **outlook** **paragraphs** help?

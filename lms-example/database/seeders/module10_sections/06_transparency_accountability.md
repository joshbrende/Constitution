[STEP] What we mean by transparency and accountability

## Transparency and Accountability

**Transparency** means **making** **information** **accessible** to **citizens** (reports, **decisions**, **performance**, **budget**, **procurement**). **Accountability** means **answerability**—**explaining** **actions**, **taking** **responsibility**, and **responding** to **citizens** and **oversight** (council, **MPAC**, **AG**). **SALGA Outcome 4** and **Outcome 5** both stress **transparency** and **accountability**; **Municipal** **Systems** **Act** and **MFMA** support **public** **access** to **certain** **information** (e.g. **PAIA**).

### Public information (examples)

| Type | What to publish / where | Typical frequency |
|------|-------------------------|-------------------|
| **IDP, budget, SDBIP** | **Website**; **libraries**; **wards**. | **Annual**; **amendments** when **adopted**. |
| **Annual report** | **Website**; **AG** **report** when **tabled**. | **Annual**. |
| **Council** **agendas** and **minutes** (non-confidential) | **Website**; **notice** **boards**. | **Per** **meeting**. |
| **Performance** **dashboards** or **summaries** | **Website**; **public** **sessions**. | **Quarterly** or **annual**. |
| **Procurement** (awards, **tenders**) | **Website**; **Government** **Tender** **Bulletin** where **required**. | **Per** **tender**/ **award**. |
| **Contact** **and** **complaint** **information** | **Website**; **bill** **inserts**; **app**. | **Ongoing**. |

[STEP] Responsiveness and feedback to citizens

## Responsiveness and Feedback to Citizens

- **Responsiveness:** **Acknowledging** **complaints** and **enquiries** **promptly**; **providing** **reference** **numbers** and **timelines**; **updating** **when** **resolved**. **Publishing** **summary** **metrics** (e.g. "90% of complaints resolved within 7 days") shows **commitment**.
- **Feedback on participation:** **Telling** **participants** how their **IDP** or **budget** **input** was **used** ("We received 50 submissions on X; we have reflected Y in the IDP"). **Closing the loop** **builds** **trust**.
- **PAIA:** **Promotion** of **Access** to **Information** **Act**—**manual**; **process** for **requests**. **POPI** **complements** (rights of **data** **subjects**). **AI** **must** **not** **expose** **personal** **data** in **published** **or** **shared** **outputs**.

[STEP] Link to engagement and AI

## Link to Engagement and AI

- **Transparency** **enables** **informed** **engagement**: **citizens** **need** **information** to **participate** **meaningfully**. **Dashboards** and **reports** (Modules 5–7) can be **published** in **summary** **form**.
- **AI:** **Chatbots** can **answer** **FAQs** about **services**, **contacts**, and **reports**—**reduce** **load** on **call** **centre**. **NLP** can **draft** **summaries** of **long** **documents** for **public** **consumption**—**you** **edit** and **approve**. **Never** **use** **AI** to **disclose** **personal** **or** **confidential** **information**.

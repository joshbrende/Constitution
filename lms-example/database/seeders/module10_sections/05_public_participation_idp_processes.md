[STEP] Legal basis — Municipal Systems Act

## Public Participation in IDP Processes

The **Municipal Systems Act** requires **municipalities** to **encourage** **community** **participation** in **municipal** **affairs**, including **IDP**, **budget**, and **performance** **management**. **Ward** **committees**, **consultation** **meetings**, **written** **submissions**, and **public** **hearings** are **common** **mechanisms**. **SALGA Outcome 4** (citizen engagement) and **Outcome 5** (active citizenry) support **meaningful** **participation**—not **tick-box**—and **responsive** **government**.

### Key obligations (high level)

| Obligation | What it means |
|------------|---------------|
| **Ward committees** | **Structures** in each **ward** to **channel** **community** **input**; **meetings**; **link** to **council** and **IDP**. |
| **IDP consultation** | **Consultation** during **IDP** **review** (annual); **notice**; **opportunity** to **submit** **written** and **verbal** **input**; **consideration** of **inputs** in **IDP** **decisions**. |
| **Budget** | **Participation** in **budget** **process** (e.g. **consultation** before **tabling**). |
| **Performance** | **Community** **input** on **priorities** and **performance** **reviews** (e.g. **SDBIP**, **annual** **report**). |

[STEP] Recording and using input

## Recording and Using Input

- **Record:** **Minutes** of **ward** **and** **consultation** **meetings**; **log** of **written** **submissions** (date, **source** (individual, **organisation**, **ward**), **summary** or **theme**, **IDP** **relevance**). **Storage** and **retention** per **policy**; **POPI** for **personal** **data** in **submissions**.
- **Consider and respond:** **IDP** **document** should **show** how **inputs** were **considered**; **council** **resolution** may **note** **key** **inputs** and **responses**. **Feedback** to **participants** (e.g. "Your submission on X was noted; we have included Y in the IDP") supports **trust**.
- **NLP of public submissions:** **Large** **volumes** of **written** **submissions** can be **summarised** and **themed** using **AI** (**NLP**)—**only** **after** **anonymising** **identifiers** if **data** goes to **public** **AI**. **In-house** **NLP** must also **comply** with **POPI**. **You** **validate** **themes** and **ensure** **no** **input** is **ignored** **because** of **automation**.

[STEP] Digital and hybrid participation

## Digital and Hybrid Participation

- **Digital:** **Online** **submission** **forms**; **online** **survey** on **IDP** **priorities**; **live-streamed** or **recorded** **consultation** **meetings**; **email** **submissions**. **Increases** **access** for **those** who **cannot** **attend** **in** **person**.
- **Hybrid:** **In-person** **meetings** **plus** **online** **participation** (e.g. **virtual** **attendees**, **online** **polls**). **Record** **both** **channels** in **one** **participation** **log**.
- **Equity:** **Not** **everyone** has **data** or **devices**. **Blend** **digital** with **ward** **meetings**, **radio**, **SMS**, **printed** **summaries**.

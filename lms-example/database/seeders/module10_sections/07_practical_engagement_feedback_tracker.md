## Practical: Engagement and Feedback Tracker

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Microsoft Excel**.

This tracker extends the **Citizen Feedback** dashboard (Module 7) to **engagement** **channels**, **complaint** **SLA**, and **link** to **IDP** **participation**. It should use **aggregated** data for **reporting**; **POPI**: no **identifiable** **citizen** data in **published** **or** **AI**-shared **outputs**. If you have a **complaint** or **CRM** **system**, this can be a **summary** **view**; if not, a **manual** or **semi-manual** **tracker**.

### Step 1: Create the workbook and sheets

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel**, new workbook.
2. Create sheets: **Channels summary**, **Complaint SLA**, **Cover**.
3. **Cover:** Title "Engagement and Feedback Tracker — [Municipality]", "Data as at [date]", "Updated: [frequency]", "Owner: [Customer Care or Communications]".

### Step 2: Channels summary sheet — structure

In the **Channels summary** sheet:

- **Row 1:** Headers: `Channel` | `Volume (period)` | `Previous period` | `Trend` | `Top 2 themes (short)` | `Status` | `Note`.
- **Rows 2–8:** One row per **channel**, e.g.:
  - *Call centre / phone*
  - *Website / app*
  - *Walk-in*
  - *Social media (monitored)*
  - *Ward committee (inputs)*
  - *IDP / consultation (submissions)*
  - *Total / overall*

- **Volume:** **Count** of **contacts**, **complaints**, **submissions**, or **inputs** in the period. **Trend:** Up, flat, down vs **previous**. **Top 2 themes:** From **anonymised** **review** or **AI**-assisted **theming** (Module 7, 10 AI). **Status:** Traffic light. **Note:** One line (e.g. "App use up; need to promote in Ward 5").

### Step 3: Complaint SLA sheet — structure

In the **Complaint SLA** sheet:

- **Row 1:** Headers: `Category` | `Received` | `Resolved` | `Within SLA %` | `SLA (days)` | `Backlog` | `Status` | `Note`.
- **Rows 2–8:** One row per **category** (Water, Roads, Billing, Other) and **Total**. **Within SLA %:** Of **resolved**, **%** within **target** (e.g. 7 days). **Backlog:** **Open** **complaints** not yet **resolved**. **Status:** Traffic lights. **Note:** One line.

- **Link** to **Module 7** **Complaints summary** if you **already** have it; **avoid** **duplication**—either **merge** or **this** sheet **feeds** that one.

### Step 4: Link to IDP participation (optional row or sheet)

- **Option A:** **Row** in **Channels summary**: "IDP / consultation (submissions)" with **volume** (e.g. **50** **written**; **12** **ward** **meetings**).
- **Option B:** **Separate** **sheet** **IDP participation** with: `Phase` (e.g. **draft** **IDP** **2025**) | `Meetings` | `Written submissions` | `Key themes (aggregated)` | `Responded (Y/N)` | `Note`. **Detailed** **IDP** **participation** **checklist** is in the **next** Practical.

### Step 5: One-sentence "What needs attention"

At the **bottom** of **Channels summary** or **Complaint SLA**: *"What needs attention?"* e.g. "Roads SLA below target; IDP submission themes to be summarised for council; promote app in under‑engaged wards." **Use** **aggregated** **wording** only.

### Use this

- **In your municipality:** **Populate** from **complaint** **system**, **call** **centre**, **ward** **and** **IDP** **logs**. **Customer Care** and **Communications** **approve** **definitions** and **traffic-light** **rules**. **Never** put **identifiable** **citizen** **data** into **public** **AI**—use **aggregated** **counts** and **themes** only.

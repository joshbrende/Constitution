## Practical: IDP Participation and Transparency Checklist

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Microsoft Excel**.

This checklist supports **compliance** with **public** **participation** (IDP, **budget**) and **transparency** **obligations**. It helps you: (1) **plan** **consultation** **phases**; (2) **record** **inputs** and **responses**; (3) **verify** **that** **information** is **published** and **accessible**. **AI** can help **suggest** **checklist** **items** from **Systems** **Act** and **good** **practice**—you **customise** and **validate** with **IDP** **coordinator** and **Legal**.

### Step 1: Create the workbook and sheets

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel** (you can add to your **Engagement** **tracker** or create new).
2. Create sheets: **IDP participation**, **Transparency**, and **Cover** (or reuse).
3. **Cover:** Title "IDP Participation and Transparency Checklist — [Municipality]", "For: [IDP 2025–30 / Annual cycle]", "Owner: [IDP Coordinator / Communications]".

### Step 2: IDP participation sheet — structure

In the **IDP participation** sheet:

- **Row 1:** Headers: `Step / activity` | `Due / done` | `Owner` | `Evidence` | `Status` | `Note`.
- **Rows 2–15:** One row per **step**, e.g.:
  - *Notice of IDP review (advert, website, wards)*
  - *Draft IDP / review document available (website, libraries)*
  - *Ward committee meetings (schedule and hold)*
  - *Public consultation meetings (schedule and hold)*
  - *Written submission period (open, close)*
  - *Online survey or form (if used)*
  - *Log of submissions (count, key themes)*
  - *Report to council on inputs (summary, consideration)*
  - *IDP adoption (council resolution)*
  - *Feedback to participants (how inputs were used)*

- **Evidence:** **Link** or **reference** (e.g. "Minutes in folder X"; "50 submissions logged"). **Status:** Done, **In progress**, **Not yet**. **Note:** One line.

### Step 3: Transparency sheet — structure

In the **Transparency** sheet:

- **Row 1:** Headers: `Item` | `Where published` | `Last updated` | `Status` | `Owner` | `Note`.
- **Rows 2–12:** One row per **item**, e.g.:
  - *IDP (current)*
  - *Budget (current)*
  - *SDBIP (current)*
  - *Annual report*
  - *Council agendas and minutes (public)*
  - *Performance summary or dashboard*
  - *Tender / procurement (awards)*
  - *Contact and complaint info*
  - *PAIA manual*
  - *Other (add)*

- **Where published:** **Website** **URL** or "Libraries, wards." **Last updated:** **Date**. **Status:** Up to date, **Needs update**, **Missing**. **Owner:** e.g. **Communications**, **CFO**, **IDP**.

### Step 4: Link to engagement tracker

- **IDP** **participation** **counts** (meetings, **submissions**) can **feed** the **Engagement** **tracker** **Channels** **summary** (e.g. "IDP / consultation"). **Transparency** **status** can **inform** **quarterly** **governance** or **communications** **reports**.

### Step 5: One-sentence "What needs attention"

At the **bottom** of **IDP participation** or **Transparency**: *"What needs attention?"* e.g. "IDP feedback-to-participants step pending; Annual report to be uploaded by Friday."

- **In your municipality:** **Align** with **Systems** **Act** and **your** **IDP** **and** **budget** **calendar**. **IDP** **coordinator** and **Legal** **approve** **steps**. **AI** can **suggest** **additional** **checklist** **items**—you **validate** and **adopt**.

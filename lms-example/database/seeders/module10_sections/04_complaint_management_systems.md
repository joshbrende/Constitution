[STEP] What is a complaint management system?

## Complaint Management Systems

A **complaint management system** **logs**, **tracks**, **routes**, and **resolves** **citizen** **complaints** (and often **compliments** and **enquiries**). It supports **responsiveness**, **accountability**, and **data** for **themes** and **improvement**. Module 7 introduced **complaints** in the **Citizen** **Feedback** **dashboard**; here we focus on **system** **design**: **logging**, **routing**, **SLA**, and **escalation**—and where **AI** (categorisation, **routing**, **chatbots**) can **support**.

### Logging and first contact

| Element | What to have |
|---------|--------------|
| **Single entry** (as far as possible) | **One** **portal**, **app**, **call** **centre**, or **walk-in** **point** that **creates** a **ticket** with **reference** **number**. **Avoid** **silos** (only **water** **log**; **roads** **never** **see**). |
| **Fields** | **Category** (water, roads, billing, etc.); **ward** or **area** (optional but **useful**); **description**; **contact** (for **resolution**—**keep** **secure**; **POPI**). **Attachments** (photo) if **supported**. |
| **Acknowledgment** | **Immediate** **acknowledgment** (SMS, **email**, **in-app**) with **reference** and **expected** **timeline**. |

[STEP] Routing, resolution, and SLA

## Routing, Resolution, and SLA

- **Routing:** **Send** **complaint** to **correct** **department** or **team** (water, roads, billing, etc.). **Rules** (e.g. "If **category** = water THEN **route** to **Water** **manager**") or **manual** **dispatch**. **AI** can **suggest** **routing** **rules** from **category** or **suggest** **category** from **anonymised** **description**—you **validate** and **implement** in **your** **system**.
- **Resolution:** **Work** **order** or **task**; **update** **status** (open, **in** **progress**, **resolved**, **closed**); **feedback** to **citizen** when **done**. **Record** **resolution** **date** and **outcome** (e.g. "Resolved—leak fixed").
- **SLA (service level agreement):** **Target** **time** to **resolve** (e.g. **critical** **water** 24h; **standard** 7 days; **billing** 14 days). **Track** **%** **within** **SLA**; **escalate** when **at** **risk**. **Dashboard** (Module 7) and **engagement** **tracker** (this module) **surface** **SLA** **performance**.

[STEP] Escalation and quality

## Escalation and Quality

- **Escalation:** **Overdue**; **citizen** **dissatisfied** with **response**; **repeat** **complaint**; **high** **profile** or **sensitive**. **Define** **who** **gets** **escalated** **complaints** (supervisor, **manager**, **MM** **office**). **AI** can **suggest** **escalation** **rules**—you **implement** and **validate** with **Customer** **Care**.
- **Quality:** **Sample** **check** **resolution** **quality**; **citizen** **satisfaction** with **complaint** **handling**. **Use** **themes** to **improve** **services** and **reduce** **recurring** **complaints**.

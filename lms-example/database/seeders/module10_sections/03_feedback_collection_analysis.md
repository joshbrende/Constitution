[STEP] Feedback collection — channels and methods

## Feedback Collection and Analysis

**Feedback** includes **complaints**, **compliments**, **suggestions**, **survey** **responses**, and **unsolicited** **comment** (e.g. **social** **media**, **emails**). Module 7 covered **satisfaction** and **complaints** for **service** **delivery** **reporting**; here we **extend** to **channels**, **analysis** **methods**, and **use** in **engagement** and **transparency** **reporting**.

### Channels (recap and extend)

| Channel | What you get | Strengths | Limitations |
|---------|--------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Surveys** (online, phone, paper) | **Structured**; **scores**; **themes**. | Comparable; **representative** if **sampled**. | Cost; **timing**; **bias** if **sample** **skewed**. |
| **Complaints / compliments** (app, web, call, walk-in) | **Structured** (category, **description**) or **free** **text**. | **Real-time**; **actionable**. | **Self-selecting**; **negative** **bias** for complaints. |
| **Social media** | **Posts**, **comments**, **mentions**. | **Early** **signal**; **public**. | **Not** **representative**; **noise**; **POPI** and **moderation** **effort**. |
| **IDP / budget consultations** | **Written** **submissions**; **verbal** **input** at **meetings**. | **Structured** **participation**; **legal** **basis**. | **Limited** **reach**; **need** to **record** and **respond**. |
| **Ward committees** | **Verbal** **input**; **sometimes** **minutes**. | **Local**; **trusted**. | **Capacity**; **consistency** of **recording**. |

[STEP] Analysis — themes, sentiment, and reporting

## Analysis: Themes, Sentiment, and Reporting

- **Themes:** **Recurring** **topics** (e.g. "water interruptions," "billing," "response time"). **Manual** **coding** or **AI**-assisted **categorisation** from **anonymised** **text**. **Taxonomy** (Module 7) helps **consistency**. **Report** **themes** as **aggregated** **counts** or **%**—**never** **identifiable** **quotes** without **consent** or **anonymisation**.
- **Sentiment:** **Positive**, **neutral**, **negative**. **AI** can **assess** **anonymised** **text**; **you** **validate**. **Report** as **aggregated** (e.g. "65% positive this quarter"). **POPI:** **Anonymise** before **public** **AI**.
- **Reporting:** **Dashboard** (Module 7 **Citizen** **Feedback**); **quarterly** or **annual** **engagement** **report**; **IDP** and **annual** **report** **sections** on **participation** and **feedback**. **Transparency:** **Publish** **summary** **metrics** (volume, **resolution**, **themes**) where **appropriate**.

[STEP] Link to performance and IDP

## Link to Performance and IDP

- **Performance:** **Feedback** **themes** and **sentiment** feed **SDBIP** and **service** **improvement** (Module 7). **Responsiveness** (e.g. **resolution** **time**) is a **KPI**.
- **IDP:** **Consultation** **input** must be **recorded**, **considered**, and **reflected** in **IDP** **decisions** (Municipal Systems Act). **Feedback** **between** **cycles** informs **reviews** and **priorities**. Your **engagement** **tracker** (Practical) can **record** **IDP** **inputs** and **responses**.

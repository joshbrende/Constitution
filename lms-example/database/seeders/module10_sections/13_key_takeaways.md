## Module 10: Key Takeaways

1. **Digital citizen engagement platforms:** **Website**, **app**, **SMS/USSD**, **social** **media**, **online** **consultation**, **chatbot**. They **complement** **face-to-face** (ward committees, imbizos); **increase** **reach** and **data** for **analysis**; **require** **equity** (blend with **non-digital**) and **governance** (ownership, **POPI**). **AI** (e.g. **chatbot**) must be **deployed** in **your** **platform**—**never** **live** **citizen** **chats** in **public** **AI**.

2. **Feedback collection and analysis:** **Channels** (surveys, complaints, **social**, **IDP** **submissions**, **ward**); **themes** and **sentiment** from **anonymised** **review** or **AI**; **reporting** in **dashboards** and **engagement** **reports**. **Link** to **performance** (Module 7) and **IDP**. **POPI:** **anonymise** before **public** **AI**; **aggregated** **only** in **reports**.

3. **Complaint management systems:** **Logging** (single entry, **reference**, **acknowledgment**); **routing** (rules or **AI**-assisted **category**); **resolution** and **SLA**; **escalation**. **AI** can suggest **routing** **rules**, **keywords** for **auto-categorisation**, and **escalation**—**implement** in **your** **system**; **anonymise** **descriptions** if **used** in **public** **AI**.

4. **Public participation in IDP processes:** **Systems** **Act**; **ward** **committees**; **consultation** (notice, **meetings**, **written** **submissions**); **recording** and **consideration** of **inputs**; **feedback** to **participants**. **NLP** of **public** **submissions** only on **anonymised** **text**; **structure** (themes, **IDP** **relevance**) can be **designed** with **AI**—**you** **own** the **process** and **POPI**.

5. **Transparency and accountability:** **Publish** **IDP**, **budget**, **SDBIP**, **annual** **report**, **council** **agendas**/ **minutes**, **performance** **summaries**, **procurement**, **contact**/ **complaint** **info**. **Responsiveness** (acknowledge, **resolve**, **report** **metrics**); **feedback** on **participation**. **AI** (e.g. **chatbot** for **FAQs**, **NLP** for **summaries**) must **not** **disclose** **personal** or **confidential** **info**.

6. **Practical skills:** You can **build** an **engagement** and **feedback** **tracker** (channels **summary**, **complaint** **SLA**, **link** to **IDP**) and an **IDP** **participation** and **transparency** **checklist** (participation **steps**, **evidence**, **transparency** **items**, **currency**). Both support **SALGA** **Outcome 4** and **5** and **compliance** with **participation** and **transparency**.

7. **AI Application—POPI and data ethics:** **Anonymise** all **citizen**, **social** **media**, and **submission** **text** before **public** **AI**. **Never** **identifiable** **data** in **ChatGPT**, **Claude**, or **public** **tools**. **Chatbots** = **your** **platform**; **routing** and **categorisation** = **your** **system**. **Validate** with **Customer Care**, **Communications**, **Legal**. **AI** **supports** **design**, **rules**, and **narrative**—**you** **own** **content**, **accuracy**, and **data** **protection**.

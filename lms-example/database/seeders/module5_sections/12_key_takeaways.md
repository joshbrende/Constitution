## Module 5: Key Takeaways

1. **Real-time vs batch:** **Batch** (daily, weekly, monthly) is right for most municipal KPI dashboards: SDBIP, collection, compliance. **Real-time** adds value when acting *today* or *this week* changes the outcome (e.g. fault backlogs, daily collection focus). Batch is simpler and easier to govern; use real-time only where the benefit justifies the cost.

2. **Dashboard design:** Start with **purpose** and **audience**. One primary audience per dashboard. Link to your **SDBIP**, **Logic Model**, and **golden thread**. **Less is more:** 5–9 KPIs per view; hierarchy (most important on top); no decoration. **Consistency:** same KPI, same definition and source; **owner** for each dashboard.

3. **KPI selection:** Use KPIs from your **SDBIP** and **data–KPI mapping** (Modules 3 and 4). Balance **leading** (early warning) and **lagging** (accountability). Weighting, when used, must be explicit and signed off. **Traffic lights** show status at a glance without implying a numeric weight.

4. **Visual analytics:** **Right chart for the job:** e.g. line for trends, bar for compare, big number + traffic light for vs target. **Traffic lights and colour:** Green/Amber/Red with clear rules; consistent meaning. **Clarity:** avoid 3D, too many decimals, clutter. Always show **“Data as at [date]”** and refresh frequency.

5. **Mobile and responsive:** MM, directors, and councillors often view on **phone or tablet**. Prefer fewer KPIs, bigger type, and a “mobile summary” view. A one-page Excel/Sheets export to PDF can work for “at a glance” on mobile.

6. **Practical skills:** You can **build a one-page KPI dashboard** (4–6 KPIs, traffic lights, one chart, one-sentence insight) in Excel or Sheets. You can **write a dashboard specification** (purpose, audience, KPIs, data source, refresh, owner, wireframe) so IT or a BI tool can implement it.

7. **AI Application:** You can use **ChatGPT** or **Claude** to: generate a **dashboard specification** and **wireframe**; suggest **KPIs and chart types** for a use case; suggest **anomaly and alert rules** and escalation; suggest **leading indicators** for early warning; and generate **one-paragraph summaries** and **“What to watch” bullets** from KPI data (NLG). **Use only aggregated, non-confidential** numbers in public AI. **Validate** all AI output with the KPI or dashboard owner.

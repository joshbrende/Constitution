## AI Application 7: One-Paragraph Executive Summary from KPI Data

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**Data and ethics:** Use only **aggregated, anonymised** KPI numbers (no person-level or sensitive project detail). You **own** and **validate** the narrative with the dashboard or report owner before use in council or SDBIP.

### Task: Turn a small KPI table into a narrative

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Paste a **non-confidential** KPI summary. Use **aggregated, anonymised** numbers only (no person-level or sensitive project detail). Example:

   > Collection rate: 71% (target 75%) — Red. Debtors 90+ days: R45m (target R40m) — Red. Revenue vs budget YTD: 98.5% — Amber. Faults resolved in 30 days: 82% (target 85%) — Amber. Performance agreements signed: 100% — Green.

3. Send this prompt:

   > Based on the KPI summary above, write a **one-paragraph executive summary** (4–6 sentences) for the MM. It should: (1) state the overall picture (e.g. "Revenue and collection need attention; governance is on track"); (2) highlight the main reds and what they mean; (3) note one or two positives; (4) end with "What needs attention" in one sentence. Use formal, municipal language. Do not add data we did not provide.

4. **Use the output:** This is a **draft** “What needs attention” or “CEO summary” for your dashboard or report. **Edit** to match your context and have the **owner** sign off before sharing. You can run this each month: update the numbers, re-paste, and get a new draft. **Do not paste confidential or person-level data.**

## AI Application 8: “What to Watch” Bullets

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Get 3–5 “what to watch” bullets from KPI status

1. In a **new chat**, send the same type of **aggregated** KPI summary (as in Application 7).
2. Send:

   > From the KPI summary above, give 3–5 short "What to watch" bullets. Each bullet: one issue or risk, in one line, with a suggested action or question (e.g. "Collection below target — review revenue campaign and 90+ prioritisation"). Be specific to the numbers we gave. Use plain language.

3. **Use the output:** Add these as a “What to watch” or “Actions” section **below** your dashboard or in the monthly pack. They help turn **status** into **action**. Validate with the KPI owner.

## AI Application 9: One-Sentence Insight for a Single KPI

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Draft one sentence that explains a KPI’s status

1. In a **new chat**, send (edit the numbers and KPI):

   > Our Collection rate this month is 71%; target is 75%. Last month it was 72%. Debtors 90+ days have increased by 8% month on month. Write **one sentence** we can put under this KPI on our dashboard to explain the status. It should say what the number is, vs target, and the main driver or concern. Plain language.

2. **Use the output:** Put this sentence in the “Note” or “Insight” column of your **Practical** dashboard (or in a tooltip). It helps readers who do not know the detail. **Check** the numbers and driver with Finance before publishing.

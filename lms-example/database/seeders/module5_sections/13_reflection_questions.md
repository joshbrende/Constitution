## Module 5: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Purpose and audience:** For one dashboard you have or want: who is the **primary audience**, and what **one question** should it answer? If you cannot answer in one sentence, how would you sharpen it?

2. **Real-time vs batch:** For your main performance KPIs (e.g. collection, SDBIP, faults), would **batch** (e.g. monthly) be enough for the decisions you make? Where would **near real-time** clearly add value?

3. **KPI selection:** Does your current or planned dashboard use KPIs from your **SDBIP** and **data–KPI mapping**? Are you balancing **leading** and **lagging** indicators? What leading indicator would you add if you had the data?

4. **Traffic lights:** Do you have **clear, written rules** for Green/Amber/Red on your main KPIs? If not, who should define them, and how would you document them?

5. **Dashboard spec:** Have you ever written a **specification** (purpose, audience, KPIs, source, refresh, owner) for a dashboard? How would it help when you hand over to IT or a new colleague?

6. **AI and NLG:** Which AI task would you use first: **dashboard spec**, **anomaly rules**, or **one-paragraph executive summary**? How would you fit it into your monthly or quarterly cycle?

## AI Application 1: Generate a Dashboard Specification

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

**Data and ethics:** Use only **aggregated** or **generic** KPI names and SDBIP-type descriptions; **never** confidential targets, person-specific data, or live system detail. You **own** and **validate** the final spec with the dashboard owner (MM, director, PMU) before use in council or SDBIP.

### Task: Get a first draft of a dashboard spec

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send this prompt (edit the [bracketed] parts to match your case):

   > We are a South African municipality. We need a one-page dashboard specification for [e.g. an Executive SDBIP overview / a Revenue KPI dashboard / a Service Delivery dashboard]. The audience is [e.g. MM and mayoral committee / Director: Finance and revenue team]. Give us: (1) a suggested name and one-sentence purpose; (2) 5–7 KPIs we should include, with a one-line definition and suggested data source for each; (3) suggested traffic-light rules (Green/Amber/Red) for 2 of those KPIs as examples; (4) suggested refresh frequency and one sentence on who should own the dashboard. Use a clear structure with headings. We will use this as a brief for our team.

3. **Use the output:** This is a **draft**. Compare to your **SDBIP** and **data–KPI mapping** (Modules 3 and 4): Do you have the data? Are the KPIs in your plan? Adjust and get the **owner** to sign off. Use it as the basis for the **Practical** spec and wireframe.

## AI Application 2: Suggest KPIs and Chart Types for a Use Case

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Get KPI and chart suggestions for a dashboard you are planning

1. In a **new chat**, send (edit as needed):

   > We are a South African municipality. We want to build a [e.g. Revenue / Service delivery / Governance and compliance] KPI dashboard for [e.g. the MM and mayoral committee / our Director and Section 56 managers]. We already report on SDBIP and have Logic Models. Suggest: (1) 6–8 KPIs we should put on the first page, with a brief reason for each; (2) for each KPI, which chart type would work best (e.g. big number + traffic light, line for trend, bar for compare) and why; (3) 2–3 leading indicators we could add for early warning. Use a table for (1) and (2); bullets for (3).

2. **Use the output:** Check against your **SDBIP** and **data–KPI mapping**. Adopt KPIs you already have; treat the rest as ideas for when data becomes available. Use the chart suggestions in your **Practical** dashboard and in the **spec**.

[STEP] AI Application 3: Dashboard layout and wireframe in text

## AI Application 3: Dashboard Layout and Wireframe in Text

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Get a text description of a dashboard layout

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > We have a one-page KPI dashboard with 6 KPIs and 1 trend chart. The KPIs are: [list them, e.g. Collection rate, Debtors 90+, Revenue vs budget, Faults resolved in 30 days, Citizen satisfaction, Performance agreements signed]. Describe a simple layout: where to put each KPI (e.g. top left, top right, grid), where to put the chart, and what the chart should show. Also suggest where to put: title, "Data as at" date, and a one-line "What needs attention." Use a short, numbered list or a simple “wireframe in words” (e.g. "Row 1: KPI1 | KPI2 | KPI3; Row 2: KPI4 | KPI5 | KPI6; Row 3: Chart; Row 4: What needs attention.").

2. **Use the output:** Turn this into your **wireframe** (Practical: Dashboard Specification). Build the layout in Excel or Sheets following this structure.

# Module 6: Financial Performance Monitoring with AI

Time Slot: 11:00 - 12:30 (Day 2)

## Purpose of This Session

This session applies the **dashboard design**, **KPI**, and **data** skills from Modules 3–5 to **financial performance**: revenue collection, budget variance, debt management, fraud detection, and financial forecasting. Municipalities face R306.7 billion in debt, a 213-day average collection period, and chronic underfunding. SALGA Outcome 5 (Inclusive Socio-Economic Return) requires effective revenue collection, elimination of fiscal leakages, and financial sustainability. You will learn **theory** on each financial area and **MFMA** context; **practical** tasks to build revenue/debt trackers and budget variance worksheets; and **AI application** tasks using ChatGPT or Claude for revenue prediction, debt prioritization, fraud red-flag rules, variance explanations, and predictive financial modeling.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

1. **Explain** revenue collection optimization: collection rate, collection period, cost to collect, and how they link to SALGA Outcome 5 and MFMA.
2. **Describe** budget variance analysis: favourable vs adverse variance, key line items, and MFMA reporting requirements.
3. **Outline** debt management strategies: ageing, irrecoverable debt, segmentation, and collection actions.
4. **Identify** fraud red flags and prevention controls in procurement, payroll, and revenue (MFMA and municipal context).
5. **Explain** financial forecasting basics: trend-based and scenario-based approaches for revenue and expenditure.
6. **Build** a revenue and debt tracker and a budget variance worksheet in Excel or Google Sheets.
7. **Use** generative AI (ChatGPT, Claude) to support revenue prediction prompts, debt prioritization rules, fraud red-flag checklists, automated variance explanations, and predictive financial modeling—with appropriate use of **aggregated, non-confidential** data only.

[STEP] How to use this module

## How to Use This Module

1. **Connect to Modules 3, 4, and 5.** Your **Logic Model** and **SDBIP** (Module 3), **data–KPI mapping** (Module 4), and **dashboard design** (Module 5) define what financial KPIs you can track and how to display them. Use them to pick KPIs and confirm data sources.
2. **Work through each step.** Use **Next** and **Previous** to move. Theory sets the concepts; **Practical** and **AI Application** give you outputs you can use in your municipality.
3. **Protect confidentiality.** In **AI Application** tasks, use only **aggregated, non-confidential** numbers (e.g. "collection rate 72%," "variance –5%"). **Never** put individual debtor names, account numbers, or personal data into ChatGPT, Claude, or other public AI tools. POPI and MFMA require care.
4. **Validate AI output.** AI can suggest rules, prompts, and structures; you and your CFO or revenue manager must validate and adapt for your context and policies.

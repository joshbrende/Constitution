## Module 8: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **MFMA compliance:** Does your municipality have a **single** **compliance** list (Section 71, 72, treasury, annual report) with **due dates** and **owners**? Who **owns** it—CFO, Risk, other? Is it **updated** when Treasury or circulars change? Would **early-warning** rules (e.g. "alert 5 days before due if not submitted") help?

2. **Legislative compliance:** Beyond MFMA, do you track **Systems Act**, **LRA**, **POPI**, **OHS**, or **other**? Is there a **compliance calendar** or **risk**-owned list? How do you **escalate** when something is **late**?

3. **Risk register:** Do you have a **risk register** with **likelihood**, **impact**, **score**, **owner**, and **treatment**? How **often** is it **reviewed**? Are **high** and **critical** risks **reported** to **council** or **audit committee**? Would **AI**-suggested **matrix** or **prioritisation** rules help—and who would **validate**?

4. **Early warning:** Do you have **defined** **triggers** (e.g. for Section 71, collection rate, fault backlog) and **escalation** (owner → CFO → MM)? Who **checks** them and how **often**? Would **AI**-suggested **trigger** and **escalation** rules help—and where would you **implement** them (Excel, BI, both)?

5. **Audit preparation:** How do you **prepare** for **AG** and **internal** audit? Do you have an **evidence** or **audit-trail** **structure** for **budget**, **Section 71/72**, **revenue**, **procurement**? Would a **generic** **audit prep** checklist (AI-drafted, then **customised** with Internal Audit) help?

6. **AI and confidentiality:** For the **AI Application** tasks: did you use **only** **generic** or **aggregated** data? What would you need (e.g. **CFO** or **Risk** sign-off, **template** for status paragraphs) to make **AI**-assisted **compliance** and **risk** **reporting** **routine** while **never** putting **confidential** data into public AI?

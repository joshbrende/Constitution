[STEP] What auditors look for

## Audit Preparation

The **Auditor-General (AG)** and **internal audit** assess **financial statements**, **performance information**, **compliance** with legislation, and **governance**. **Preparation**—ongoing, not last-minute—reduces **findings**, **qualifications**, and **disclaimers**. **SALGA Outcome 3** (Capable Local Government) and **clean audits** are linked; your **compliance** and **risk** work in this module directly supports **audit readiness**.

### What auditors typically assess

| Area | What they look for | How your trackers help |
|------|--------------------|-------------------------|
| **Financial** | AFS; supporting records; revenue, expenditure, assets, liabilities. | **Budget variance**, **revenue/debt** (Module 6); **documentation** of decisions and reconciliations. |
| **Performance** | Reliability of **performance info** (SDBIP, annual report). | **Logic Models**, **SDBIP** linkage, **data–KPI mapping** (Modules 3–4); **dashboards** and **trackers** (5–7) show **source** and **"Data as at."** |
| **Compliance** | MFMA, **legislation**, **treasury**, **SCM**. | **Compliance tracker** (this module): due dates, submitted, **evidence**. |
| **Governance** | Council, **MPAC**, **delegations**, **risk**, **internal control**. | **Risk register**; **early warning**; **delegations** register; **MPAC** resolution tracking. |

[STEP] Documentation and evidence

## Documentation and Evidence

- **Documentation:** **Policies**, **procedures**, **delegations**, **minutes**, **reconciliations**, **approvals**. Auditors **sample** and **trace**. Your **compliance** tracker should **link** each obligation to **where** the **evidence** is (e.g. "Section 71 Jan — folder X; Treasury acknowledgment in email").
- **Audit trail:** **Who** approved **what**, **when**. **AI** can help **design** an **audit-trail** or **evidence-index** **structure**—you **populate** and **store** in **your** systems. **Never** put **confidential** audit working papers or **AG** drafts into public AI.

[STEP] Ongoing vs pre-audit

## Ongoing vs Pre-Audit

- **Ongoing:** **Compliance** and **risk** monitoring **through the year** = **audit readiness**. If Section 71 is **always** on time, **reconciliations** are **current**, and **risk** is **reviewed**, the **pre-audit** scramble is smaller.
- **Pre-audit:** **Checklist** of **what** AG or internal audit will request; **gaps** filled **before** they arrive. Your **compliance** and **risk** owners (CFO, Risk, Internal Audit) typically lead. **AI** can help **generate** a **generic** "audit preparation checklist" from **public** AG and treasury guidance—you **customise** and **validate** with your **audit** liaison.

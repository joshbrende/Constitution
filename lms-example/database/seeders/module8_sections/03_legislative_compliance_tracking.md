## Legislative Compliance Tracking

Besides **MFMA**, municipalities must comply with a range of **national** and **provincial** legislation. A **legislative compliance calendar** or **tracker** helps you: (1) know **what** is due **when**; (2) assign **owners**; (3) **escalate** when late or at risk; (4) support **audit** and **oversight**. **SALGA Outcome 3** (legal compliance, by-law enforcement) and **audit** expectations make this essential.

### Key legislation (examples)

| Legislation | What to track | Examples of obligations |
|-------------|---------------|--------------------------|
| **MFMA** | Budget, Section 71/72, annual report, SCM. | See Module 8 theory on MFMA. |
| **Municipal Systems Act** | IDP, performance management, community participation, staff systems. | IDP adoption; SDBIP; ward committees. |
| **Municipal Structures Act** | Council, committees, delegations. | Council meetings; MPAC; delegations register. |
| **Labour Relations Act (LRA)** | Collective agreements, disputes, fairness. | Bargaining; CCMA; discipline. |
| **POPI** | Personal information; processing; consent; security. | PAIA; data handling; consent where needed. |
| **Occupational Health and Safety** | Workplace safety. | Incidents; inspections; training. |
| **Other** | Sector laws (water, environment, etc.); by-laws. | Varies by municipality. |

## Compliance Calendar and Tracker

- **Compliance calendar:** A **list** of **obligations** with **due dates** (monthly, quarterly, annually). For each: **Obligation** | **Legislation / source** | **Due date** | **Owner** | **Submitted / done (Y/N, date)** | **Status** | **Note**.
- **Refresh:** Update when **new** circulars or laws add obligations; when **due dates** shift. **Risk** and **Legal** often own or feed this.
- **Escalation:** **Amber** if due soon and not yet done; **Red** if overdue. **Early warning** (later in this module) can **alert** when a due date is approaching and owner has not confirmed.

[STEP] Link to audit and oversight

## Link to Audit and Oversight

- **Auditor-General:** Checks **MFMA**, **annual report**, **financial statements**, **performance**, and **compliance** with key laws. A **compliant** and **documented** process reduces **findings**.
- **MPAC (Municipal Public Accounts Committee):** Oversight of **finance**, **audit**, and **performance**. Your **compliance** and **risk** trackers support **MPAC** briefings.
- **CoGTA / Provincial:** May require **specific** returns or **explanations**. Your **calendar** should include these when applicable.

## AI Application 4: Compliance and Checklist Support

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task A: Generate an MFMA or governance checklist

1. In a **new chat**, send:

   > We are a South African municipality. Give us a checklist of 10–12 key MFMA-related submission and compliance items we should track for the financial year (e.g. Annual Report, budget, SDBIP, mid-year report, audit). For each item: (1) name, (2) typical deadline or frequency, (3) who is usually responsible (role, not name), (4) one line on why it matters. Use a table. Focus on things a municipal manager or CFO would monitor.

2. **Use the output:** This is a **starting point** for your own compliance register. You would cross-check with your MFMA practitioner, legal, or Treasury, and tailor to your municipality's calendar and structure. It illustrates how generative AI can help you build or refresh checklists quickly.

### Task B: Extract actions and dates from a council report excerpt

1. Paste a short, **non-confidential** excerpt from council minutes or a committee report that contains resolutions or actions (or use this synthetic example):

   > Council resolved that the Municipal Manager must table the draft IDP by 15 March; that the CFO must submit the mid-year budget report by 31 October; and that the Director: Community Services must report on the status of the waste contract by the next Council meeting in April.

2. Send:

   > From the text above, list each action or resolution. For each: (1) what must be done, (2) who is responsible (role), (3) deadline or timing. Use a table.

3. **Use the output:** This supports **tracking implementation** of council resolutions—a common KPI and governance requirement. You would verify against the actual minutes and your project or performance system.

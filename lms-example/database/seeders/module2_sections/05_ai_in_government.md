## AI in Government Globally and in South Africa

Governments are increasingly using AI to improve services, compliance, and decision-making. Examples include:

- **United Kingdom**: Use of NLP to triage and route Freedom of Information requests; analytics for fraud and error in benefits.
- **Estonia**: AI-assisted document processing and chatbots for citizen queries.
- **South Africa**: CoGTA, National Treasury, and some municipalities are exploring AI for compliance monitoring, revenue, and data integration. SALGA’s focus on **Customised Innovative Solutions** (Outcome 2) and digitising M&E creates a clear mandate to build these capabilities.

For municipal officials, the message is: **AI in government is real, regulated, and relevant**. This course gives you the foundation to participate in that shift in a responsible, practical way.

# Module 2: AI Fundamentals for Public Sector

Time Slot: 11:00 - 12:30

## Purpose of This Session

This session gives you the **conceptual and practical foundation** for using AI—especially **generative AI**—in your daily work. You will learn what AI is in plain language, how it differs from traditional analytics, and how to use tools like ChatGPT and Claude safely and effectively in a municipal context. The focus is on the **practical side**: you will complete hands-on tasks that you can apply immediately to drafting, analysing documents, supporting compliance, and understanding citizen feedback.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

1. **Explain** in simple terms what AI is—including Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and generative AI—and how each applies to public sector performance management.
2. **Distinguish** between traditional (rules-based) analytics and AI-driven approaches, and when to use each.
3. **Use** generative AI tools (e.g. ChatGPT, Claude) to draft briefs, summarise documents, extract commitments from plans, and support checklists—with appropriate regard for ethics and POPI.
4. **Apply** basic prompts to analyse non-confidential text (e.g. citizen feedback, policy excerpts) for themes and sentiment.
5. **Describe** ethical AI principles and POPI-aware practices for municipal use of public AI tools.

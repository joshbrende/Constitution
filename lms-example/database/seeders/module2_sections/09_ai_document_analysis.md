## AI Application 2: Document and Policy Analysis

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Extract commitments and suggest KPIs from a planning excerpt

1. In a **new chat**, paste this sample (or a **non-confidential** IDP or SDBIP paragraph—remove names, specific projects, or sensitive data):

   > Our municipality will refurbish 15 km of roads in the next financial year, connect 400 new households to water, and reduce water losses by 6%. We will also train 25 staff in project management and complete the asset register update by the end of Q2. We aim to improve our revenue collection rate from 68% to 75% and to reduce the average debt collection period.

2. Send this prompt:

   > From the paragraph above: (a) list each commitment as a separate bullet; (b) for each, say if it is an OUTPUT (something we deliver) or an OUTCOME (a result or change); (c) suggest one simple measure or target we could put in a SDBIP (number, %, or date). Use a table with columns: Commitment | Type (Output/Outcome) | Suggested measure.

3. **Use the output:** This is a starting point for your own KPI design. In Modules 3 and 4 we will go deeper on indicator design and data. Here, the aim is to see how generative AI can **structure** planning text into a format that supports performance management and alignment with SALGA outcomes.

### Optional: Check alignment with SALGA outcomes

4. Add a follow-up message:

   > For each commitment in your table, which of the six SALGA strategic outcomes (1: Agile Force, 2: Customised Innovative Solutions, 3: Capable Local Government, 4: Effective Networks, 5: Inclusive Socio-Economic Return, 6: Developmental Growth) does it most closely support? Add a column "SALGA outcome" to the table.

5. **Use the output:** This helps you position your KPIs when reporting to council, CoGTA, or SALGA, and when aligning your SDBIP to the IDP and national priorities.

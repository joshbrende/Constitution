## Building Your Own Prompt Library

As you use generative AI in your work, **save prompts that work** for your role. For example:

- *"Summarise this [type of document] in 5 bullet points for an executive."*
- *"From this IDP paragraph, list commitments and suggest one KPI each."*
- *"Draft a one-page brief for [committee] on [topic], with problem, proposal, and ask."*
- *"Generate an MFMA / governance / [theme] checklist for a South African municipality."*

Keep a simple document or spreadsheet: **Prompt | Purpose | Example output or tweaks**. Tailor prompts for your municipality (e.g. "district" vs "metro"), and iterate. In later modules we will connect these practices to performance frameworks, data, and dashboards.

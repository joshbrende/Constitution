## Module 3: Reflection Questions

Before proceeding to the Knowledge Check, consider:

1. **Results chain:** For one programme in your municipality, can you name at least one output and one outcome? Are they clearly different?

2. **Golden thread:** Can you trace one of your SDBIP KPIs back to an IDP priority and to a budget line? If not, what information do you need to complete the link?

3. **Indicators:** Do you have indicators that are mislabelled (e.g. an outcome called an output)? How would you fix them?

4. **Logic Model:** Would a Logic Model for your directorate’s main programme help with council or audit discussions? What would you put in the Outcomes column?

5. **AI:** Which of the AI tasks in this module would be most useful in your role—extracting indicators, generating a Logic Model, or drafting alignment text? How would you fit it into your planning cycle?

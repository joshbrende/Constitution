## Practical: IDP–SDBIP Linkage Table

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Excel**.

### Step 1: Choose one IDP priority or objective

From your IDP, pick **one** strategic priority or objective (e.g. “Improve access to basic services,” “Strengthen financial sustainability,” “Enhance governance and compliance”). If you do not have the IDP at hand, use a **generic** example for practice: *“Improve access to reliable water and sanitation.”*

### Step 2: Create the table

Set up a table with these columns (you can add or omit depending on what you use):

| IDP priority / objective | SDBIP objective | SDBIP KPI | Indicator type | Budget vote / line | Owner (role) |
|-------------------------|-----------------|-----------|----------------|--------------------|--------------|
| | | | Output / Outcome | | |

### Step 3: Fill the row(s)

- **IDP priority:** The exact or paraphrased IDP objective.
- **SDBIP objective:** The annual objective that implements it (from your SDBIP).
- **SDBIP KPI:** The measurable KPI (e.g. “500 new water connections,” “Collection rate 75%”).
- **Indicator type:** Output or Outcome.
- **Budget vote/line:** The vote or line that funds this (e.g. “Vote 8: Water,” “Capital: Water R X m”).
- **Owner:** The role (e.g. “Director: Technical Services,” “CFO”).

### Step 4: Add a second row if one IDP priority has more than one SDBIP KPI

One IDP objective often has **several** SDBIP KPIs (e.g. connections, km of pipe, water quality). Each can be one row. The IDP priority repeats; SDBIP objective and KPI change.

### Step 5: Write a one-sentence “golden thread” summary

Below the table, write **one sentence** that states the link. Example: *“Our IDP priority to improve access to water is implemented through SDBIP objective X and KPIs Y and Z, funded under Vote 8, and owned by the Director: Technical Services.”*

[STEP] Golden thread one-pager (optional)

## Optional: Golden Thread One-Pager for a Directorate

If you are a **directorate head** or **performance officer**, you can turn the linkage into a **one-pager** for your directorate:

1. **Heading:** *“Golden thread: [Directorate name] — [Financial year].”*
2. **IDP priorities we implement:** 2–4 bullets from the IDP that your directorate is responsible for.
3. **SDBIP objectives and KPIs:** List the SDBIP objectives and KPIs for your directorate. For each, note the IDP priority it supports.
4. **Budget:** One line: “Our programmes are funded under Vote X (R Y m).”
5. **Individual scorecards:** “Section 56/57 agreements in this directorate cascade from the above SDBIP KPIs.”

**Use this:** The one-pager helps in **audit prep**, **council briefings**, and **SALGA/CoGTA** reporting. It makes the thread explicit.

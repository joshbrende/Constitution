## District Development Model (DDM): One Plan, One Budget, One Approach

The **District Development Model** is a national initiative to improve coordination across the **three spheres** of government (national, provincial, local) and between **district** and **local** municipalities. The aim is **One Plan, One Budget, One Approach** in a district: aligned planning, shared priorities, and less duplication.

### Why it matters for your performance framework

- **SALGA Outcome 3** (Capable Local Government) and **Outcome 4** (Effective Networks and IGR) explicitly support DDM. Your IDP and performance systems should show how you **align** with the district and with provincial/national priorities.
- **IDP**: Your IDP must take into account the **district spatial development plan** and any **One Plan** or district priorities. DDM alignment is often a standing item in IDP and SDBIP reviews.
- **Reporting**: CoGTA and provinces may ask how your municipality is supporting the DDM. Your SDBIP and annual report can include a short narrative or checklist on DDM alignment.

[STEP] What to do in practice

## What to Do in Practice: DDM and Your IDP

| Step | What you can do |
|------|------------------|
| **1. Know the district plan** | Get the district municipality’s **One Plan** or equivalent. Identify priorities that affect your local municipality (e.g. bulk infrastructure, shared services, economic nodes). |
| **2. Map your IDP to district priorities** | In your IDP, or in an annex, show which of your **objectives** or **projects** support district priorities. A simple table: *Our IDP objective* | *Relevant district priority* | *How we support it*. |
| **3. Reflect in SDBIP** | Include at least one **KPI or objective** that reflects DDM alignment (e.g. “Participate in X district forums”; “Implement Y project in line with district One Plan”). |
| **4. Report** | In your **annual report** or **oversight submissions**, briefly describe how you have contributed to the DDM. |

### Hands-on: DDM one-pager

1. Open a Word doc or **Google Doc**.
2. Head it: *“DDM alignment — [Your municipality] and [District name].”*
3. In a short table or bullets, list: (a) **2–3 district priorities** that affect you, (b) **2–3 of your IDP objectives** that support them, (c) **one SDBIP KPI or activity** that you will report on.
4. **Use this:** Share with your IDP coordinator or manager. This can become an annex to the IDP or a standing slide for council and oversight.

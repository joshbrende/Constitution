## AI Application 4: Generate an IDP–SDBIP Linkage Checklist

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Create a linkage checklist for your municipality

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send this prompt (edit the [type] if you wish: metro, district, local):

   > We are a [local/district/metro] municipality in South Africa. We need a practical checklist to verify that our IDP, SDBIP, and budget are properly linked (the "golden thread"). Give us 10–12 yes/no questions we can use in our next planning or audit prep session. For each question: (1) what we are checking, (2) what a "yes" looks like, (3) where we would find the evidence (e.g. IDP chapter 3, SDBIP, budget vote). Use a table. Focus on linkage, not on the content of each document.

3. **Use the output:** In your next **IDP** or **SDBIP** review—or in **audit preparation**—work through the checklist. Tick “yes” or “no.” The “no” answers are gaps to fix (e.g. an SDBIP KPI with no clear IDP parent; a budget line with no SDBIP objective).

## AI Application 5: Draft Alignment Text to IDP and SALGA

**Tool:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

### Task: Generate a short “alignment narrative” for a KPI

When you report to council, CoGTA, or SALGA, you often need to **state how a KPI links to the IDP and to a SALGA outcome**. AI can draft a first version.

1. In a **new chat**, send (edit the bracketed parts):

   > I need a one-paragraph narrative for our annual report. Our SDBIP includes this KPI: [e.g. "500 new household water connections by 30 June"]. Write a short, formal paragraph that: (1) states the KPI, (2) explains how it links to our IDP (assume our IDP has a priority to improve access to basic services), and (3) states which SALGA strategic outcome it supports (use the six SALGA 2026 outcomes) and in one sentence how. Use professional, municipal language. Do not include specific numbers except the KPI.

2. **Use the output:** This is a **template** for your annual report or oversight submissions. Replace “assume our IDP has…” with your actual IDP wording. Have your manager or IDP coordinator sign off before publishing.

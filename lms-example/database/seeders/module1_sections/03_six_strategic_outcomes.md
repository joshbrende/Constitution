[STEP] Outcomes 1 & 2: Agile Force and Customised Solutions — Overview and hands-on

## Outcome 1: SALGA as an Agile Force of Influence

**Key requirements:** Strengthen capacity; develop leadership with systems thinking; measurable value-add and clean audits; improve advocacy, lobbying, and labour relations; profile as a professional body with validated data.

**Outcome 2: Customised Innovative Solutions**

**Key requirements:** Digitise planning and M&E; manage and interpret big data; develop and standardise technology for local government; establish research capability and lessons-learned portals.

## Hands-on: Use ChatGPT or Claude for advocacy and KPI extraction

**Tools:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai) (free tiers are enough).

### Task A — Draft an advocacy brief (Outcome 1)

1. Open **ChatGPT** (chat.openai.com) or **Claude** (claude.ai) and sign in.
2. In the message box, paste this prompt (edit the bracketed parts for your municipality):

   > You are helping a South African municipality align with SALGA's Outcome 1 (Agile Force of Influence). Draft a one-page advocacy brief we can use in IGR engagements. Include: (1) three specific capacity gaps we are addressing, (2) two systems-thinking initiatives we are implementing, and (3) how we will provide validated data to SALGA. Use simple, professional language. End with three concrete "asks" for provincial or national support.

3. Press **Send**. Review the output.
4. Add a second message: *"Add a short paragraph on how we will improve labour relations in the municipality, and one measurable target for the next 12 months."*
5. **Use this in practice:** Copy the result into a Word or Google Doc. Edit it with your real projects and share it with your manager or IGR lead.

### Task B — Extract KPIs from a planning-style paragraph (Outcome 2)

1. In a **new chat**, paste this sample text (or use a real excerpt from your IDP or APP — remove confidential details):

   > Our municipality will improve access to water by expanding the pipeline to Ward 7, reduce the average debt collection period from 213 days to 180 days, and train 20 staff in data analytics. We will digitise our SDBIP reporting and set up a lessons-learned register by Q2.

2. Then send this prompt:

   > From the paragraph above, list each commitment as a separate KPI. For each one, give: (a) a short title, (b) one possible measure (number, percentage, or date), and (c) whether it fits SALGA Outcome 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6. Use a simple table format.

3. **Use this in practice:** Apply the same prompt to a real paragraph from your IDP or SDBIP. Use the table to check that your KPIs are linked to SALGA outcomes.

## Hands-on: Spreadsheet performance checklist and AI policy-alignment list

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** (sheets.google.com) or **Microsoft Excel**; **ChatGPT** or **Claude**.

### Task A — Simple performance checklist (Outcome 3)

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel** and create a new sheet.
2. In **row 1** put: `Area` | `Requirement (from Outcome 3)` | `Our action` | `Owner` | `Due date` | `Status`.
3. In the **Area** column, add: *Leadership development*, *Results-based planning*, *Compliance & by-laws*, *Performance management system*, *DDM alignment*.
4. In the **Requirement** column, in your own words, write one sentence per row for what SALGA expects (e.g. for *Compliance & by-laws*: "Ensure we meet MFMA and by-law enforcement requirements").
5. In **Our action**, write one concrete step your municipality will take in the next 6 months.
6. Leave **Owner**, **Due date**, and **Status** to fill in with your team. **Use this:** This becomes a living checklist for Outcome 3. Update it in your SDBIP or APP review.

### Task B — Policy alignment checklist (Outcome 4)

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send:

   > List 10 questions we can use to check if our municipality's IDP and key policies are aligned with SALGA Outcome 4 (Effective Networks and IGR). For each question, say what we are checking (e.g. IGR participation, DDM, PPPs) and what a "yes" answer looks like. Use a numbered list.

3. Copy the list into a Word or Google Doc. **Use this:** In your next IDP or policy review, work through the questions and tick which ones you can answer "yes". The "no" answers are gaps to address.

## Hands-on: Revenue/debt tracker sketch and LED opportunity summary

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Excel**; **ChatGPT** or **Claude**.

### Task A — Top 5 revenue and debt indicators (Outcome 5)

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel**, new sheet.
2. Create two sections: **Revenue** and **Debt**.
3. Under **Revenue**, in column A type: *Total billed*, *Collection rate %,* *Collection period (days)*, *Cost to collect (as % of revenue)*, *Main revenue sources (list)*.
4. Under **Debt**, type: *Total debt*, *Debt older than 90 days*, *Irrecoverable debt (estimate %)*, *Top 3 debtor categories*, *Actions taken this quarter*.
5. In the next column, leave space for **Current value** and **Target**.
6. **Use this:** Fill in with real numbers (from your budget or CFO). If you don't have a number, write "Not yet tracked". This becomes the start of a simple dashboard for Outcome 5. In later modules we will add AI and automation.

### Task B — LED opportunity summary (Outcome 6)

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Send (replace with your municipality name and one or two real facts):

   > Our municipality is [name]. We want to improve local economic development (LED) and attract investment, in line with SALGA Outcome 6. We have [e.g. an industrial park / a township economy / agricultural land / a tourism asset]. In 4 short bullet points, suggest: (1) one concrete way to make the business climate more attractive, (2) one reindustrialisation or green economy idea that fits our context, (3) one rural or township economy opportunity, and (4) one simple first step we can take in the next 3 months. Use plain language.

3. Copy the bullets. **Use this:** Share with your LED or planning unit. Use it to start a short LED action list for your IDP or APP.

## Turn outcomes into a self-assessment

## Hands-on: 6-outcome self-assessment with ChatGPT or Claude

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat.
2. Paste this prompt (you can add your municipality name and sector):

   > I work for a South African municipality. We need a simple self-assessment against SALGA's six strategic outcomes for 2026. For each outcome, give: (1) the outcome name, (2) one "we are on track" sign, (3) one "we need to improve" sign, and (4) one suggested first step. Use a table with 4 columns. Base this on typical municipal challenges: debt, revenue collection, compliance, capacity, IGR, and LED.

3. **Use this:** Fill the table in with your team. For each outcome, mark whether you are "on track" or "need to improve". The suggested first steps can go into your performance plan or risk register.

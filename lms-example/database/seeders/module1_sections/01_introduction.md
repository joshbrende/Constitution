# Module 1: Understanding SALGA 2026 Context

Time Slot: 09:00 - 10:30

## Purpose of This Session

This session gives you the **policy and strategic context** without which performance management—and the AI-enabled systems covered later—lacks direction. SALGA's 2025–2026 framework defines what "good" looks like for planning, financial management, service delivery, and compliance. Your KPIs, dashboards, and oversight systems must measure progress toward these. This module ensures you know what to measure and why.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

1. **State** why SALGA's 2026 framework matters for your municipality's performance management and where it fits in the intergovernmental system.
2. **Name** the six strategic outcomes and the performance requirements and result areas they create for municipalities.
3. **Describe** the critical municipal challenges (debt, revenue, service delivery, digital capacity, governance) and the key figures that make AI-enabled oversight urgent.
4. **Explain** how alignment with the MTSF and integrated planning (IDP, SDBIP, DDM) fits into SALGA 2026 and your performance system.
5. **Identify** where your municipality stands relative to these requirements and where AI can add value in later modules.

## How to Use This Module as a Manual

1. **Treat each outcome and each challenge as a lens.** In the next sections we will do **hands-on tasks** with ChatGPT, Claude, and spreadsheets. Use the "Key Requirements" and "Result Areas" as a checklist for your municipality.
2. **Map your municipality.** As you go through each **step** (use **Next** and **Previous** to move between steps), note where your municipality is strong, where it is weak, and where AI could add the most value.
3. **Connect to your documents.** Relate what you learn to your IDP, SDBIP, budget, and APP. The hands-on outputs (briefs, checklists, KPI tables) are meant to be used in your real plans.
4. **Prepare for AI.** This module mixes context with **practical tasks**. In Modules 2–12 we will go deeper on tools and automation. Here we lay the "what" and "why" and start the "how".

## Bottom Line

SALGA 2026 is not optional context; it defines the standards and priorities your performance system must reflect. This session gives you the structure and the figures you need to design and justify KPIs, dashboards, and—later—AI-enabled oversight. **Value and benefits** are aligned with **all six SALGA strategic outcomes** (Agile Force, Customised Innovative Solutions, Capable and Reputable Local Government, Effective Networks and IGR, Inclusive Socio-Economic Return, Developmental Growth) and with the SALGA 2025–26 APP and mandate. **Ethics** in performance, reporting, and data use are addressed throughout. Use this module as the reference for the rest of the course.

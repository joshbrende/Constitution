[STEP] Introduction — Six outcomes, one per step

# 2. SALGA's Six Strategic Outcomes for 2026

This lesson is organised as **one outcome per step**. Use **Next** and **Previous** to move between outcomes. Each outcome includes Key Requirements, Result Areas, and a **practical AI Application** with specific tools and step-by-step instructions.

---

[STEP] Outcome 1: SALGA as an Agile Force of Influence

## Outcome 1: SALGA as an Agile Force of Influence

**Key Requirements:**

- Strengthen capacity and unite political and administrative capabilities
- Develop leadership with systems thinking approach
- Provide measurable value-add while maintaining clean audit findings
- Harness business efficiencies and growth opportunities
- Improve advocacy skills, lobbying capacity, and labour relations expertise
- Profile as professional body providing validated data and knowledge

**Result Areas:** Inclusive leadership and team development; Innovative decision-making; Financial viability

---

### AI Application — Practical steps

**Tools:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai) (free tiers are enough)

1. Open **ChatGPT** (chat.openai.com) or **Claude** (claude.ai) and sign in.
2. Start a **new chat**. In the message box, paste this prompt (edit the bracketed parts for your municipality):

   > You are helping a South African municipality align with SALGA's Outcome 1 (Agile Force of Influence). Draft a one-page advocacy brief we can use in IGR engagements. Include: (1) three specific capacity gaps we are addressing, (2) two systems-thinking initiatives we are implementing, and (3) how we will provide validated data to SALGA. Use simple, professional language. End with three concrete "asks" for provincial or national support.

3. Press **Send**. Review the output.
4. Add a second message: *"Add a short paragraph on how we will improve labour relations in the municipality, and one measurable target for the next 12 months."*
5. **Use in practice:** Copy the result into Word or Google Docs. Replace placeholders with your real projects and share with your manager or IGR lead.

[STEP] Outcome 2: Customised Innovative Solutions

## Outcome 2: Customised Innovative Solutions

**Key Requirements:**

- Digitise planning, monitoring and evaluation processes
- Develop standards for local government (Redbook approach)
- Manage and interpret big data to benefit municipalities
- Develop and standardise appropriate technology for LG
- Revise relevant legislation and policies
- Establish research capability and lessons learned portal

**Result Areas:** Customer-centric digital transformation; Evidence-based decision making (Research); Revised processes and standards (Redbook); Knowledge sharing

---

### AI Application — Practical steps

**Tools:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a **new chat**.
2. Paste this sample text (or use a real, non-confidential excerpt from your IDP or APP):

   > Our municipality will improve access to water by expanding the pipeline to Ward 7, reduce the average debt collection period from 213 days to 180 days, and train 20 staff in data analytics. We will digitise our SDBIP reporting and set up a lessons-learned register by Q2.

3. Then send this prompt:

   > From the paragraph above, list each commitment as a separate KPI. For each one, give: (a) a short title, (b) one possible measure (number, percentage, or date), and (c) whether it fits SALGA Outcome 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6. Use a simple table format.

4. **Use in practice:** Apply the same prompt to a real paragraph from your IDP or SDBIP. Use the table to check that your KPIs are linked to SALGA outcomes and to spot gaps in digitisation or M&E.

[STEP] Outcome 3: Capable and Reputable Local Government

## Outcome 3: Capable and Reputable Local Government

**Key Requirements:**

- Build capacity for skilled and competent leadership
- Develop leadership (political and administrative) with results-based planning
- Embed systems thinking for problem-solving
- Practice ethical leadership
- Improve employer-employee relationships
- Ensure legal compliance and by-law enforcement
- Standardise recruitment-to-retirement processes
- Implement remunerative performance management system
- Ensure rigorous political oversight
- Investigate powers and functions to create functional municipal model
- Support District Development Model (DDM)

**Result Areas:** Capacitated local government; Skilled and competent leadership; Sound local government decision-making; Revised legislative frameworks, policies and assumptions; Clarified powers and functions

---

### AI Application — Practical steps

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** (sheets.google.com) or **Microsoft Excel**; [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel** and create a new sheet.
2. In **row 1** put: `Area` | `Requirement (from Outcome 3)` | `Our action` | `Owner` | `Due date` | `Status`.
3. In the **Area** column, add: *Leadership development*, *Results-based planning*, *Compliance & by-laws*, *Performance management system*, *DDM alignment*.
4. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a new chat. Send:

   > For a South African municipality, for each of these areas—Leadership development, Results-based planning, Compliance and by-laws, Performance management system, DDM alignment—give one sentence that describes what SALGA Outcome 3 expects. Use plain language.

5. Copy each sentence into the **Requirement** column of your sheet.
6. In **Our action**, write one concrete step your municipality will take in the next 6 months for each row. Leave **Owner**, **Due date**, and **Status** for your team to fill.
7. **Use in practice:** Treat this as a living checklist for Outcome 3. Update it in your SDBIP or APP review.

[STEP] Outcome 4: Effective Networks and IGR

## Outcome 4: Effective Networks and IGR

**Key Requirements:**

- Actively participate in intergovernmental relations (IGR) structures
- Provide common policy positions driven by members' mandates
- Establish strategic networks and public-private partnerships
- Support integrated planning (IDP/DDM)
- Coordinate delivery across three spheres of government
- Reduce red-tape and coordinate policy/legislation development

**Result Areas:** Functional IGR structures and systems; Coordinated networking and stakeholder relations; Strategic partnerships; Integrated planning (IDP, DDM); Municipal legislative compliance enforcement

---

### AI Application — Practical steps

**Tools:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a **new chat**.
2. Send:

   > List 10 questions we can use to check if our municipality's IDP and key policies are aligned with SALGA Outcome 4 (Effective Networks and IGR). For each question, say what we are checking (e.g. IGR participation, DDM, PPPs) and what a "yes" answer looks like. Use a numbered list.

3. Copy the list into Word or Google Docs.
4. **Use in practice:** In your next IDP or policy review, work through the questions and tick which ones you can answer "yes". The "no" answers are gaps to address. Share the checklist with your IGR or planning unit.

[STEP] Outcome 5: Inclusive Socio-Economic Return

## Outcome 5: Inclusive Socio-Economic Return

**Key Requirements:**

- Support appropriate financial planning
- Source equitable and adequate share from intergovernmental transfers
- Implement effective revenue collection instruments
- Develop alternative revenue streams
- Ensure effective and efficient utilisation of allocated financials
- Eliminate fiscal leakages and inefficiencies
- Practice accountability, transparency and good financial governance
- Achieve spatial justice through aligned municipal spatial development plans
- Improve local government services and infrastructure
- Ensure equitable and sustainable access to quality basic services

**Result Areas:** Spatial justice; Improved access to reliable and quality services; Social cohesion and active citizenry; Financially viable Local Government

---

### AI Application — Practical steps

**Tools:** **Google Sheets** or **Microsoft Excel**; [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

1. Open **Google Sheets** or **Excel**, new sheet. Create two sections: **Revenue** and **Debt**.
2. Under **Revenue**, in column A type: *Total billed*, *Collection rate %*, *Collection period (days)*, *Cost to collect (as % of revenue)*, *Main revenue sources (list)*.
3. Under **Debt**, type: *Total debt*, *Debt older than 90 days*, *Irrecoverable debt (estimate %)*, *Top 3 debtor categories*, *Actions taken this quarter*.
4. In the next column, add **Current value** and **Target** for each row.
5. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude**. Send:

   > For a South African municipality, suggest 3 simple internal controls to reduce fiscal leakages, in line with SALGA Outcome 5. For each: name, one-sentence description, and who typically owns it (e.g. CFO, Audit). Use a short bullet list.

6. **Use in practice:** Add the three controls to your checklist or risk register. Fill the Revenue and Debt rows with real numbers from your budget or CFO. If you don’t have a figure, write "Not yet tracked". This becomes the start of a simple dashboard for Outcome 5; later modules add AI and automation.

[STEP] Outcome 6: Developmental Growth for Global Competitiveness

## Outcome 6: Developmental Growth for Global Competitiveness

**Key Requirements:**

- Create conducive business climate to attract and retain investments
- Revive existing industrial infrastructure (reindustrialisation)
- Position local government as global investment destination
- Identify, package and develop strategies for rural, township and regional economies
- Replicate Building Inclusive Green Municipalities (BIGM) project
- Accelerate Green Economy initiatives as alternative revenue generation

**Result Areas:** Global economic investment in municipal areas; Improved business climate; Improved Local Economic Development; Green economy

---

### AI Application — Practical steps

**Tools:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)

1. Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** in a **new chat**.
2. Send (replace with your municipality name and one or two real facts):

   > Our municipality is [name]. We want to improve local economic development (LED) and attract investment, in line with SALGA Outcome 6. We have [e.g. an industrial park / a township economy / agricultural land / a tourism asset]. In 4 short bullet points, suggest: (1) one concrete way to make the business climate more attractive, (2) one reindustrialisation or green economy idea that fits our context, (3) one rural or township economy opportunity, and (4) one simple first step we can take in the next 3 months. Use plain language.

3. **Use in practice:** Copy the bullets. Share with your LED or planning unit. Use them to start a short LED action list for your IDP or APP. In later modules we will use AI for demand forecasting and business climate dashboards.
